Officers were dispatched to the WORT radio station shortly after 3:00 am this morning reference a shooting.  One person was injured, after a firearm was discharged, and they suffered non-life threatening injuries.  The victim was conveyed to a local hospital for treatment.  The Madison Police Department Violent Crime Unit (VCU) responded to the scene, and are actively investigating this incident.  It appears that this incident was not random, and there is no risk to the public.  The Violent Crime Unit is asking that anyone with information, regarding this incident, please contact either the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers (608) 266 - 6014.  Anyone providing information, that leads to an arrest, can remain anonymous.
