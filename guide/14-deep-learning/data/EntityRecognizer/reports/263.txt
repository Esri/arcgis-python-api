MPD officers responded to the intersection of S Stoughton Rd and E Broadway for a motor vehicle versus pedestrian crash. The pedestrian, a 48-year-old female, was crossing S Stoughton Rd and was struck by a vehicle. The vehicle left the area and has not been located. The pedestrian was conveyed to a local hospital for medical treatment and is described as being in stable condition with injuries primarily to her lower extremities. Investigation is on-going. If anyone has information about this incident, please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crimestoppers. Released 02/18/2018 at 9:15 PM by Lt. Mindy Winter
