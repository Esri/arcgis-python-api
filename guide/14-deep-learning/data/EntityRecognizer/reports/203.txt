A 59 year old, Madison woman was crossing E. Washington Ave, on foot, when she was struck by a pickup truck that was fitted with a plow.  The pedestrian was transported to the hospital with non-life threatening injuries.  The driver of the pickup truck remained on scene and was not injured.  The pedestrian will be issued a citation for sudden pedestrian movement and will be admitted to the hospital. Released 01/02/2016 at 8:25 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
