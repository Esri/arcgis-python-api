 A 23-year-old Madison man was arrested during a high-risk traffic stop on Barton Rd. Thursday afternoon after the MPD received information that he had just stolen a handgun from a car that was parked at the BP gas station, 699 S. Gammon Rd. The victim, the gun's owner, has a concealed carry license and is an acquaintance of the suspect. He told police he saw the suspect take the weapon from his car. It has not been recovered. Released 03/10/2017 at 10:16 AM by PIO Joel Despain
