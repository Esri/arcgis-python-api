A 35-year-old Madison man contacted the MPD Monday afternoon to report a robbery he said took place inside his Randolph Dr. apartment around 11:00 p.m. Sunday night. The victim said three men pushed their way into his home, and that he was knocked down and repeatedly punched and kicked. He had no visual injuries. The man said an electric guitar was taken by the intruders. He was also missing his wallet and cell phone. Investigators believe he was specifically targeted.
