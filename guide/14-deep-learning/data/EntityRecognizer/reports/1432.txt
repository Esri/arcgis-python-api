On 4/21/18 at approximately 8:16 PM Madison police officers were sent to the area of the Cannonball bike path on Madison's south side for what was determined to be a robbery.  While walking in the area the victims were attacked by multiple unknown suspects.  During the course of the robbery the victims were battered and had money taken.  A K9 track was attempted but officers were unable to locate the suspects.  MPD's investigation into this incident is continuing. Released 04/22/2018 at 1:08 AM by Sgt. Kurt Wege
