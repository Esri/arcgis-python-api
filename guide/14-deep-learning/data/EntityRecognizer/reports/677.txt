The MPD located a stolen Chevy truck on N. Blount St. around 1:30 a.m. this morning and arrested – at gunpoint - a suspect who was inside. There were a number of items found inside the truck that also appeared to be stolen. The suspect claimed he was only sleeping in the truck. He was arrested for being a passenger in a stolen vehicle. The truck belongs to a downtown Madison property manager. He had left it unlocked and running early Wednesday morning while working inside a residence on E. Gilman St.  Released 10/25/2018 at 10:06 AM by PIO Joel Despain
