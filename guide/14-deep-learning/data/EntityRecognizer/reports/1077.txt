 Numerous callers reported an out an out control man running in traffic on University Ave. near Highland Ave. Tuesday evening. The 20-year-old was trying to get into occupied cars. He jumped on one car, and moments later was banging on the door of an apartment building while screaming something about drugs. Officers located the man and struggled to bring him under control. He said:" "Let's do drugs," after letting officers know he "took acid." He also said he was sorry as officers took him to a hospital to get help. The man will be cited for disorderly conduct and resisting. Released 11/22/2017 at 1:44 PM by PIO Joel Despain
