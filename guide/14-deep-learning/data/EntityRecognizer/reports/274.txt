 An inappropriate relationship, that was fostered through social media, has ended with the arrest of a 33-year-old Madison man. The suspect is accused of having a sexual relationship with a child over the age of 16, and exposing her to harmful materials. The victim, who is 17, said sex acts with the suspect took place earlier this month. He was arrested yesterday following an investigation by the MPD's Special Victims Unit.
