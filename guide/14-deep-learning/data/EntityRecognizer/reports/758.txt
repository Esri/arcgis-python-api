On 01/01/2018 at approximately 4:30 PM an unknown white male robbed the Bassett Food Mart. The suspect is described as a white male, with a tall and thin build, dressed in dark clothing. The investigation is on-going and the Madison Police Department is looking into the possibilty that this is the second armed robbery committed on this date by the same suspect.  Released 01/02/2018 at 2:48 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
