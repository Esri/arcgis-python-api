The Madison Police Department was contacted by a 23 year old female who reported she was shot at at the intersection of S. Whitney Way and Schroeder Road.  The victim was in her car at the time and was stopped at a red light.  She was not injured.  Evidence was located that confirmed a shooting took place.  There were two possible suspect vehicles that were observed, one a gray or black newer SUV, and the other a red SUV with an "8" in the registration plate. This incident is still under investigation.  If anyone has any information regarding this incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.  Released 10/05/2018 at 1:17 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
