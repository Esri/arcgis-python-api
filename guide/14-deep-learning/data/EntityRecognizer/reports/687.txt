At approximately 7:20 pm, the Communications Center received multiple calls of shots fired in area of Fisher St and Center St. Officers responded into the area and found the scene of the shooting in the 2000 block of Fisher St. At about the same time, a victim arrived at St Mary's hospital with an injury that is not life threatening. Officers responded to the hospital. There is no indication that this victim was the intended target of the shooting. Officers remain on scene and are talking to others who may be involved. This does not appear to be a random act. Released 10/11/2017 at 9:29 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
