Madison Police Department Officers were traveling in their marked squad on Pennsylvania Ave. A green van driven by the suspect pulls out from Third Street at a high rate of speed. The suspect vehicle strikes the side of the officers squad and flees the scene. Further investigation provided a suspect and vehicle information. At approximately 10:21 p.m. officers locate the suspect as well as his vehicle on Melvin Ct and Ridgeway Ave. Hamilton was arrested for Hit and Run, Possession of Drug Paraphernalia, Carrying a Concealed Weapon and several outstanding warrants. MPD officers did not sustain any injuries.  Released 01/23/2019 at 4:27 AM by Lt. Reginald Patterson
