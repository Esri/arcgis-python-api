The Madison Police Department responded to the Red Rock Saloon this morning regarding a stabbing.  The victim sustained a non-life threatening injury.  The victim is not aware of how he was stabbed, and the investigation is continuing. If anyone has any information regarding this incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program. Released 09/01/2018 at 2:49 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
