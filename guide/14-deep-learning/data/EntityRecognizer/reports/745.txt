A Middleton teen, walking on Pflaum Rd. to a relative's home Saturday night heard a popping sound. He soon discovered his head was bleeding. He went to a hospital and a pellet was found to be lodged in his scalp. The victim said he did not see anyone shooting a pellet gun, and is not aware of anyone who would want to harm him. He was treated and released. Released 03/14/2016 at 11:26 AM by PIO Joel Despain
