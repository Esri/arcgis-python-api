There was a report of shots fired near the corner of Todd Dr and Ardsley Ln..  Officers located multiple casings outside and observed that several rounds appeared to have struck a building on the 2900 bl Todd Dr..  One of those rounds entered into an apartment.  The resident was not home and it doesn't appear anybody was injured.  At this time there is no suspect info and the investigation is continuing.  Released 09/15/2016 at 3:51 AM by Lt. John Radovan
