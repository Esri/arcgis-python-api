 The MPD took several (three) reports of damaged car windows in the area of W. Lakeside St. Thursday morning. Residents reported windows of parked vehicles had been shattered by a BB or pellet gun. Besides W. Lakeside St., homeowners on Gilson St. and Van Deusen St. also reported similar vandalism. Released 03/11/2016 at 9:53 AM by PIO Joel Despain
