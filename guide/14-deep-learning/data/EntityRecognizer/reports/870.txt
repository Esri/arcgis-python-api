Victim stated he was on foot somewhere near Sycamore Avenue when he was approached and jumped by 3 males.  Victim believed he was struck by a bat or metal bar, knocked on unconscious, and his wallet was taken.  Victim could not provide an exact location or time of this incident.  He was transported to an area hospital by EMS for evaluation and treatment of his injuries.
