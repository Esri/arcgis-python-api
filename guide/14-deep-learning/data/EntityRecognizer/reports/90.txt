Madison Police responded to the Staybridge Suites for an individual reportedly shot.  After securing the scene an injured subject was located who was transported by MAFD to a local hospital with a gunshot wound.  The suspect or suspects in this shooting were not on scene when officers arrived. The investigation is being coordinated by the Madison Police Departments Violent Crime Unit and is ongoing at this time.   Released 04/08/2017 at 2:29 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
