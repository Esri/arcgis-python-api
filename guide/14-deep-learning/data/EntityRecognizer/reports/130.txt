On 12-22-18, officers were dispatched to Gulseth Street, reference a residential burglary. The home owner looked in his garage when he saw two people going through his vehicle. The home owner chased the suspects and was able to catch one and hold that suspect down until police arrived on scene. At one point, the home owner saw one of the other suspects holding something long that he thought was a gun. That suspect was yelling at the home owner to let his friend go which the home owner did not. Officers arrived on scene and took the one suspect in custody where he was charged with party to a crime of residential burglary. The other suspect fled and is at large. Released 12/23/2018 at 12:25 AM by Sgt. Ryan Gibson
