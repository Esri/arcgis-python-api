On 1/26/18 at approximately 11:05 PM the Madison Police Dept. was dispatched to the area of Knutson Dr. and Heffernan Dr. on Madison's north side for multiple calls of shots being fired.  A suspicious vehicle was seen leaving the area around the time of this incident.  Officers, a sergeant, and an investigator responded and located casings in the street.  A nearby, and unoccupied, residence was struck by one of the bullets.  Contact was made with those residents by police.  At this time there are no injuries reported with this incident.  MPD's investigation into this incident continues. Released 01/27/2018 at 12:37 AM by Sgt. Kurt Wege
