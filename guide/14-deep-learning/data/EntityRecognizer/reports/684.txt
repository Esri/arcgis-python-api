Officers were called to the parking lot of the Walmart on Nakoosa Trl for a report of an individual slumped over in the driver's seat of a vehicle with the engine running. Madison Fire was able to wake the individual, who displayed signs of intoxication. Officers also observed several open containers of alcohol within the vehicle. The individual was ultimately arrested for operating a motor vehicle while intoxicated-6th offense. He was taken to the hospital for an evidentiary blood draw before being taken to the Dane County Jail and booked under the above offense. Released 11/08/2017 at 10:22 AM by Lt. Anthony Bitterman
