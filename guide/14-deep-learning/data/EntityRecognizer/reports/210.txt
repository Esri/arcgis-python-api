Witness contacted security at Meriter Hospital and reported gunshots on South Park St.  Officers located 3 shell casings in the south bound lanes of 1400 S. Park St.  Witness told officers that he saw a white SUV and a brown sedan driving south on Park St. shooting at each other.  No indication of injuries to the persons involved or property damage. Released 10/13/2017 at 4:26 AM by Sgt. David Compton
