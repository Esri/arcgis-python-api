 A 21-year-old homeless man was just putting his sleeping bag down in the 100 block of State St. last night when he came under attack. The victim said he was punched multiple times in the head by a man who ran his pockets so aggressively, he ripped one open. The suspect took the victim's cell phone and fled. A passerby witnessed the violence and called police. Officers determined the victim had suffered significant head injuries and was taken to a hospital. Utilizing surveillance images, they were able to identify and later arrest the suspect in the 100 block of E. Mifflin St. Released 10/20/2017 at 12:04 PM by PIO Joel Despain
