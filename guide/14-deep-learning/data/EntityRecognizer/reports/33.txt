 Madison Police responded to multiple reported shots fired calls throughout the overnight hours.   Just before 2:00 am a house was struck with one bullet in the the 1000 blk of East Johnson Street but no injuries were reported. On the south side of Madison near Turbot Drive multiple callers reported shots fired and a person running from the scene about 11:45 pm.  No injuries or damage were located in this incident. On the east side of Madison, at Milwaukee Street near Rosemary Ave officers also responded to shots fired about 10:30 pm but no damage or injuries were reported. If you have any information about any of these weapons offenses please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers. Released 01/23/2017 at 5:02 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
