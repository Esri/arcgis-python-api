 " She could have easily pocketed the money and no one would have been the wiser, except the poor woman who lost her wallet – who, as you can imagine, was thrilled beyond belief to get her wallet and money back!" The comment came from the MPD's property room supervisor this week after she successfully reunited a 23-year-old Madison woman with her wallet and more than $2,000 contained therein. A 59-year-old Madison woman found the wallet near E. Main St. and First St. last Friday and turned it into the MPD. She knew how much cash was in it, but that didn't make any difference. Said the property room supervisor: "I admire her honesty and integrity!" Released 08/12/2016 at 10:49 AM by PIO Joel Despain
