At approximately 1:25 AM the Stop 'n Go convenience store located in the 2000 block of Winnebago St. was robbed.  The robbery was reported by the clerk who stated the suspect displayed a handgun and demanded money.  She was then ordered to wait in a back room while the suspect fled.  The clerk was not injured during this incident.  The suspect was described as a dark complected black male in his late 20's to early 30's.  He was estimated to be 5'8" tall and was wearing a mask and dark colored clothing.  A K9 track was completed but officers were unable to locate the suspect.  The investigation into this incident is ongoing. Released 10/27/2018 at 3:14 AM by Sgt. Kurt Wege
