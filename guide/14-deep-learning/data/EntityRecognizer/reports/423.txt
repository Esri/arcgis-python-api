The victim, a 26 year old male, was parked in his vehicle in the parking lot in the 600 block S Whitney Way.  Victim stated he had money in his lap that he was counting out for a potential purchase from a nearby store.  Victim state an unidentified male then approached him with a knife and robbed him of the cash.  Suspect then fled in a vehicle toward the Beltline.  No injuries reported. Released 10/19/2016 at 4:24 AM by Lt. Anthony Fiore
