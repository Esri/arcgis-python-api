At approximately 2:00 AM the Madison police department was sent to the 100 block of S. Butler St for the reports of shots being fired.  Officers arrived to dozens of subjects fleeing the area.  Officers were able to determine that some sort of disturbance occurred in front of a bar in that area.  During the course of that disturbance a shot was heard and people scattered.  At this point MPD's investigation hasn't located any injured parties or damage to nearby property.  The investigation into this incident continues. If anyone has any information regarding this incident, they are asked to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.   Released 12/02/2018 at 3:57 AM by Sgt. Joseph Engler
