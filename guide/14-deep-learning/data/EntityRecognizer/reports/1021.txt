Multiple callers reported hearing multiple rounds in the 3800 block of Sycamore Avenue.  Officers arriving on scene located shell casings in the street on this block, and soon thereafter discovered multiple rounds had struck an occupied residence.  Officers made contact with the residents and at this time no one is believed to be injured as a result of this incident.  The street has been temporarily shut down to thru traffic, and the investigation is continuing. Released 09/28/2017 at 4:38 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
