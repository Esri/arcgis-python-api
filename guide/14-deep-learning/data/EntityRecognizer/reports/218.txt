On Wednesday, detectives from the MPD's Violent Crime Unit, Burglary Crime Unit and the Dane County Narcotics Task Force assisted Chicago Police detectives in apprehending two Madison area brothers both of whom are wanted on homicide warrants out of Cook County, Illinois. Jordan L. Sharlow, age 26 and Justin J. Sharlow, age 25, were taken into custody separately, both at locations in Madison. MPD detectives are also looking at the Sharlow brothers for possible involvement in local weapons offenses.    Released 01/19/2018 at 1:47 PM by PIO Joel Despain
