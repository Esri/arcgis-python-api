 The MPD responded to reports of multiple gun shots being heard in the 700 block of N. Oak St. early Sunday morning. Officers recovered several shell casings, and determined they had come from at least two different guns. There were no immediate reports of injuries or property damage. One witness saw a dark colored sedan speeding from the area following the reports. Released 10/03/2016 at 10:22 AM by PIO Joel Despain
