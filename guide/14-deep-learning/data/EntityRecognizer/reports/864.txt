 MPD officers were working a seatbelt enforcement grant yesterday near the intersection of Highway 30 and Highway 51. The driver of a pickup truck was observed not wearing a seatbelt and was pulled over. The arresting officer noted the smell of alcohol coming from the cab as well as empty beer cans and an empty alcohol bottle in the truck's bed. The driver was found to be intoxicated and was arrested for his fifth offense of drunken driving. Released 06/30/2017 at 9:15 AM by PIO Joel Despain
