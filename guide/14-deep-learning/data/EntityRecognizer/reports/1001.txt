Madison Police officers were near the intersection of Francis Street and State Street when they heard a gunshot and observed a vehicle exiting the Lake Street ramp (on the Frances St. side) at a high rate of speed.  This vehicle was found by other officers and stopped.  Investigation at the ramp did yield evidence of a shooting there immediately following a disturbance.  The lone occupant of the vehicle has been arrested for his participation in this incident. This incident is still under investigation.  The Madison Police are encouraging those who were present at this scene and/or witnessed it to contact the Madison Police Department.  Released 08/10/2018 at 4:20 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
