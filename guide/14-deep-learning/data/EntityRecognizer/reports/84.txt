Monday afternoon, the Dane County Narcotics Task Force seized nearly 330 grams of cocaine during a traffic stop near Stoughton, on Highway 51. A Chicago man had been pulled over for speeding. He and his passenger were arrested for possession with intent to deliver. More than $1,000 cash was also confiscated.  Released 07/13/2018 at 12:17 PM by PIO Joel Despain
