Employees from Potbelly's Sandwich Shop were closing up the store for the night when the above suspects forced their way inside.  One displayed a handgun and brought the manager into the back room to the store's safe.  Both suspects left a short time later with an undisclosed amount of money in an unknown direction.  There were no reported injuries as a result of this incident, and the investigation continues. Released 04/02/2017 at 12:58 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
