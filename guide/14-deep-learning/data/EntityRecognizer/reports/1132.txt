At approximately 3:19 PM, MPD received a report of a disturbance or fight behind Falk Elementary School, involving adults and younger persons.  During this fight, there was a report of a single gunshot being fired.  On arrival, officers located an 18 year old male with an apparent gunshot wound.  He was transported to an area hospital with non life-threatening injuries.  MPD personnel are still investigating, and several people have been detained pursuant to this investigation.  MPD does not believe that this is a random incident. Released 07/28/2016 at 5:38 PM by Sgt. Edward Marshall
