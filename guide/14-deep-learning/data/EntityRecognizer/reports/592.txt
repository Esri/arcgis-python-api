Victim reports that he was going to his vehicle which was parked in a parking lot for a business in the 2900 (block) E Washington Ave.  Victim stated he was approached by an unknow male subject who asked the victim if he had a 'lighter'.  The suspect then produced a silver semi-automatic handgun and robbed the victim of his cellphone and a small amount of cash.  Suspect then fled in a white Cadillac (unknow model).  No additional information available at this time.
