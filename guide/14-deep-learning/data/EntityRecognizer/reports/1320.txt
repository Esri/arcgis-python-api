 A Burberry Dr. resident said she was hugged and touched inappropriately in the chest after a stranger insisted on helping her carry groceries into her apartment building. Before entering her unit, the woman took the groceries back from the man as both stood in a common hallway. He responded with the unwanted physical contact. The woman was able get inside her apartment and lock the door, but said the man stood outside it for several minutes asking to come in. Officers were unable to locate him. Released 03/30/2016 at 11:36 AM by PIO Joel Despain
