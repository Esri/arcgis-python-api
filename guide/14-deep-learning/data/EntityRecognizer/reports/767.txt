A Madison teen reports being mugged in the 4300 block of Nakoosa Trail Wednesday evening. The victim said he was walking near an apartment complex when a gunman approached and escorted him to the side of a building. That is where two accomplices waiting. One served as a lookout while the other ran the victim's pockets. The trio made off with the victim's cash and cell phone. He could not provide good descriptions of the men who robbed him.  Released 12/18/2015 at 9:56 AM by PIO Joel Despain
