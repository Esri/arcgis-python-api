 A 20-year-old man reported being robbed by several men Sunday night after he got inside a car with them. The victim said he arranged to meet one of the men as they are acquainted with each other on social media. He said the robbery took place Oakbridge Court with the person he knows and three others pulling a total of five guns on him. When asked how four robbers used five guns, he said one had a pistol in each hand. The victim said he lost his cell phone, cash and his own handgun in the holdup. He said his gun is real but not functional.   Released 05/13/2019 at 2:55 PM by PIO Joel Despain
