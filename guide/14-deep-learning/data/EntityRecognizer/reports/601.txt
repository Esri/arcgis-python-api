West District officers were dispatched to the 6200 block of Schroeder Rd for a report of shots being fired. Eight shell casings were recovered from the roadway, but there was no immediate report of injuries. One projectile penetrated a residence in the 6200 block of Strathmore Lane and that projectile caused property damage only. Released 01/21/2018 at 8:29 PM by Lt. Mindy Winter
