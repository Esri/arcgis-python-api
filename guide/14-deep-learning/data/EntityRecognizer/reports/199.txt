Madison Police are looking for assistance in locating a missing adult. Hilda Vergara Lago was last seen in the 3100 block of Clove Dr. at approximately 12:30pm. Hilda is 88 years old and left home to take a walk. This is not out of the ordinary, except that she has been gone for an unusually long time and is fairly new to the area. Hilda was last seen wearing a white hat and a blue jacket. Hilda speaks Spanish. If you see Hilda, please contact the Dane County Communications Center by calling the non-emergency number 255-2345 or calling 911. Released 10/29/2017 at 3:50 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
