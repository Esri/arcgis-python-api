Two teens - both males - fled from a damaged and stolen SUV after one drove the Chevrolet Traverse into the back end of a car that was operating on S. Gammon Rd. Tuesday afternoon. There were no injuries, but the victim's car ended up on a nearby lawn.  Witnesses told police the reckless driver and his passenger ran off on Park Ridge Dr. An officer found the Traverse, its doors wide open, still running and stopped in a traffic lane. It was listed as stolen out of Cottage Grove.  Released 06/05/2019 at 10:01 AM by PIO Joel Despain
