The Madison Police Department responded to the 1000 block of E. Dayton Street for a vehicle that struck a house and a gas meter causing a gas leak. Officers evacuated a one block radius of the crash and blocked surrounding streets. MG&E and Madison Fire Department responded to the scene. The gas leaked was stopped after approximately fifteen minutes. After the investigation residents were allowed back in their homes. There were no injuries reported. The driver was arrested for First Offense OWI, Possession of Drug Paraphernalia, Seat Belt Violation and Failure to Maintain Control. Released 01/18/2019 at 2:23 AM by Lt. Reginald Patterson
