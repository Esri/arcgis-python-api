 A teenager suffered a likely broken leg after being hit by a car on E. Washington Ave. late this morning. The driver told police he was eastbound and had slowed for a red light at First St. The signal changed to green and he began to accelerate. The 17-year-old was running in the crosswalk and was struck. The crash is under investigation and no citations have yet been issued. Released 04/14/2016 at 2:49 PM by PIO Joel Despain
