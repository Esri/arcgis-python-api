MPD officers responded to a report of possible shots fired in the area of the 4200 block of Lumley Rd. Responding officers stopped and detained several vehicles, but all occupants were found to have not been involved in the shots fired incident. Officers located several spent shell casings at the scene and located at least one building with possible bullet strike damage. No injuries were reported as a result of this incident. Released 05/06/2018 at 8:01 PM by Lt. Mindy Winter
