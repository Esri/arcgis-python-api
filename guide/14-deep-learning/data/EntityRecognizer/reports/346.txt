Madison Police Officers responded to a Strong Armed Robbery that occurred at the Family Dollar, 2849 N Sherman Ave. The suspect grabbed the clerk, threw them to the ground, and took money from the register.  The suspect fled on foot with an undisclosed amount of currency. Anyone with information about this crime is encouraged to call Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 608-266-6014. Released 04/01/2019 at 8:45 PM by Lt. Scott Kleinfeldt
