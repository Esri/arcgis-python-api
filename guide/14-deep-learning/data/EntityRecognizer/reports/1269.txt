On 05/15/2017 at about 456pm Madison Police Officers responded to multiple reports of shots fired in the area of Beld St and Bram St in the South Police District.  Upon arrival officers did not locate any injured parties, victims,  or suspects at the scene.  Three buildings and at least one vehicle in the area were struck by gunfire.  The impact locations on the buildings included living spaces.  Several bullet casings were recovered in the area.  A K-9 track was conducted without result.  The Madison Police Department Violent Crime Unit will be conducting the ongoing investigation. Released 05/15/2017 at 8:51 PM by Sgt. Michael Richardson
