Officers responded to the report of 3-4 shots fired. Upon arrival they located an unoccupied vehicle park on the street that had been struck by several rounds. It appears the suspect(s) left the area in a vehicle. There were no injuries or other damage located. Anyone with information on this crime is urged to call the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014. Released 04/24/2016 at 11:03 AM by Lt. Timothy Strassman
