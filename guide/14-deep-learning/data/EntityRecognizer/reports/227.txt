Officers were contacted by the victim, who is a delivery driver for a local restaurant.  The driver was making a delivery to an address in the 6500 block of Raymond Rd.  The victim was contacted by three suspects who attempted to steal his wallet. The victim fled, but tripped and fell and was assaulted by the suspects.  The suspects fled with some of the victim's belongings. The description provided by the victim was very generic.  The three suspects were described as teenaged black males, wearing jeans and shirts that may have had hoods.  Released 04/01/2017 at 7:11 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
