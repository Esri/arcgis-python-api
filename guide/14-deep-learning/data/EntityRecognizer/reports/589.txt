At approximately 3:45 am, officers were dispatched to the area of 6700 Jacobs Way, for a report of shots fired.  Officers received information that there were several suspicious people gathering in the area prior to the shooting.  One vehicle did sustain damage.  No injuries have been reported.  There is no suspect information. Released 02/11/2017 at 4:08 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
