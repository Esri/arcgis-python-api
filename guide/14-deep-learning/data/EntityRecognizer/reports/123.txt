At approximately 4:00pm several calls to 911 came in reporting a vehicle shooting at another vehicle near this intersection.  Witnesses reported seeing a black handgun being fired from from a green older model car at a black car. Witnesses then reported these vehicles fleeing the area at a high rate of speed.  Officers responded and began searching for the involved vehicles and persons.  Officers located six bullet casings at the scene.  After an extensive search, the vehicles were not located.  At this time, no victims have been located and officers are checking with area hospitals. Investigation is continuing. Released 11/28/2017 at 6:42 PM by Sgt. Matthew Baker
