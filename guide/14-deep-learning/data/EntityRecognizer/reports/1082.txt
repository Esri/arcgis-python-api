 Officers were sent twice Sunday night to Sunfish Court after people reported hearing gunshots. On the first trip, around 8:45. p.m., officers could find no evidence of gunfire. However, on the second call, at 10:26 p.m., they did recover a casing. There were no reports of injuries or property damage. Released 02/25/2019 at 8:31 AM by PIO Joel Despain
