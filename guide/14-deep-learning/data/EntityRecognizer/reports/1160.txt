The Madison Police Department was called to Sherman Terrace this morning reference an armed robbery.  The two victims were walking to their vehicle when they were contacted by the above described suspect.  That suspect took out a handgun and demanded money and property from the victims.  The suspect was given that property, which included the keys to the vehicle the victims were about to enter.  The suspect then took that vehicle.  The victims were not injured. The stolen vehicle is a gray 2011 Hyundai Tucson with IL license plates. If anyone has any information regarding this incident, or if someone sees this vehicle, please contact the Madison Police Department as soon as possible.  Released 10/06/2018 at 4:17 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
