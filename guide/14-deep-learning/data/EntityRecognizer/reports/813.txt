On 11/20/16 at approximately 8:57 PM, the Madison Police Department responded to the 1600 block of Wright St., for a report of a male who had been shot.  The victim was transported to a local hospital and is currently in critical condition.  The suspect remains at-large.  The Madison Police Department believes that this was a targeted shooting and not a random act of violence.  Anyone with information is asked to contact Crime Stoppers at 608-266-6014.    Released 11/21/2016 at 3:41 AM by Sgt. Shannon Blackamore
