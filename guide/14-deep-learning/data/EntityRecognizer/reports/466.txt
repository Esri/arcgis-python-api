 The MPD responded to a car versus street sign crash at the intersection of S. Park St. and Delaplaine Court Monday afternoon. The driver was uninjured but unconscious and still behind the wheel. The first officer on scene was able to open the driver's door and safely get him out of the car. He was arrested for his fourth offense of drunken driving.
