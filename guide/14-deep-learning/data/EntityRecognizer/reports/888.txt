Two young men reported an armed carjacking Wednesday night - while a third victim fled the scene prior to police arrival. The victims said they pulled into the parking lot of a Coho St. apartment building to meet someone they only knew by a street name. A gunman approached the car and ordered the victims out. He also told told them to empty their pockets and leave contents on the seats. The victims ended up losing cash and the car they came in after the gunman drove off in it. Police found the car a couple of hours later parked near a West Beltline Highway business. The suspect was gone, but there was marijuana and drug paraphernalia inside. The victims said the contraband did not belong to them. Detectives believe this was a targeted robbery. Released 09/08/2016 at 10:58 AM by PIO Joel Despain
