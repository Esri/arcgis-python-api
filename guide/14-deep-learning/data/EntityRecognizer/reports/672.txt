MPD officers were dispatched to 1862 Beld St. reference a man with a knife.  The victim reported that the suspect grabbed him by the shirt, took his cell phone and jewelry.  The suspect was known to the victim.  A knife was not observed or mentioned during the incident.  The suspect left prior to police arrival.  The suspect is at large.
