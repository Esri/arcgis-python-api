Madison Police were dispatched to the area of 200 South Bassett Street following multiple reports of what were believed to be shots fired.  Calls began coming in to the 911 Center at approximately 1:50 am. Although no evidence, damage to property nor injuries to persons were reported, the consistent observations of several callers indicates that shots were likely fired.  Several witness statements indicate that these potential shots rang out in two distinct bursts. MPD continues to investigate. Anyone with additional information is asked to call 255-2345.  Year to date, 13 cases of shots fired have been reported in the City of Madison.  This on the heels of 220 reported cases in 2017.  Released 01/26/2018 at 11:01 AM by P.O. David Dexheimer
