 A burglary victim's home surveillance cameras have led to the identification and arrest of one of two suspects who broke into the 27-year-old's Temkin Ave. home on July 22nd.  The victim returned that night to find the residence ransacked. Numerous electronics had been stolen. Entry had been gained via a window. Utilizing images captured inside the residence the MPD developed probable cause to take one of the men into custody. He was arrested, without incident, yesterday.  Released 08/10/2016 at 11:16 AM by PIO Joel Despain
