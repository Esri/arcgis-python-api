At 22:06 Madison Police Officers were called to an armed robbery at the 7-Eleven near Todd Drive and the Beltline.  In this incident a male came into the store with a handgun and demanded money.  The suspect then left on foot with an unknown amount of cash. The store shut down for the night after this armed robbery.  No injuries were reported during this incident. The suspect was described as a mixed race or black male, early 20's, 6'01", wearing a blue hoodie, light colored pants and a white rag over his face.  If you have any information about this armed robbery please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers.  Released 10/09/2017 at 2:36 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
