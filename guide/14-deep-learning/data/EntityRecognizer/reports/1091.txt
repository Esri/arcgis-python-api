An attack in a shopping center parking lot Thursday morning sent one woman to a hospital and the other to jail. The suspect and victim are acquainted with each other and bumped into one another outside the S. Whitney Way Walgreens. The injured woman said the suspect charged and punched her in the head. She was also pulled to the ground by the hair, dragged, and battered some more. She was treated for facial injuries.  Released 01/22/2016 at 11:27 AM by PIO Joel Despain
