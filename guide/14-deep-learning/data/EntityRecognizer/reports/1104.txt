During the afternoon of 4/22, two events occurred in the Central District - the March for Science and the Peoples Climate March.  Both events took place without incident, and no arrests, injury or damage was reported.  Approximately 5,000 people attended the March for Science and 2,500 took part in the Peoples Climate March.  MPD Special Events Team, Patrol and other personnel worked to facilitate a safe event. Released 04/22/2017 at 6:28 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
