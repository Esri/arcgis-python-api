 A Madison man has been arrested for his fourth offense of operating while impaired following a rollover on Junction Rd. Sunday morning. The driver had two children in his car as the time of the crash. When the car came to a rest, the man and the children ran from the scene, but were quickly located by officers. The driver told police he fell asleep behind the wheel. There were no serious injuries. Released 05/07/2019 at 10:10 AM by PIO Joel Despain
