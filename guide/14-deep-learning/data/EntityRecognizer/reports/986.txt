 A Madison woman suffered life-threatening stab wounds while traveling in a car with a suspect – a Madison man - with whom she is acquainted. She is in surgery. The woman got out of the car near the bike path at John Nolen Dr. and Broom St.  The MPD got the call at 12:15 p.m. Before officers arrived, the suspect drove off and is at large. The MPD's Violent Crime Unit along with Central District detectives and patrol officers are currently working the case. The motive for the violence is not known at this time.  Released 11/10/2017 at 2:23 PM by PIO Joel Despain
