 Madison Police responded to the 5100 blk of Great Gray Drive after several callers reported multiple shots fired and a male subject running from the area.  Officers responded and found one casing.  An unknown vehicle was heard driving from the scene just after the shooting.  No injuries or property damage was located after the area was canvassed.If you have any information about this shooting please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers.    Released 12/01/2016 at 12:52 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
