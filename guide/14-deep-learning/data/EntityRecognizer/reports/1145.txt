The MPD is seeking citizen help in locating a missing 15-year-old girl. Haylee Akers left her Jacobs Way home several days ago. She has been known to frequent the West Towne Mall area, as well as State Street. There is no evidence she has met with foul play, but there is concern for her welfare. Anyone with knowledge of Haylee's whereabouts is asked to contact the MPD's non-emergency number at (608)255-2345. Released 07/25/2018 at 11:09 AM by PIO Joel Despain
