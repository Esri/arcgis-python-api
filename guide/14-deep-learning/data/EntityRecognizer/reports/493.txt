 The MPD is looking to identify three persons of interest after a 97-year-old woman's wallet was stolen from her purse while she shopped at Metcalfe's, 726 N. Midvale Blvd., last Thursday. The victim's credit cards were used to purchase more than $5,000 worth of gift cards at area Target and Home Depot stores. Anyone with information is asked to contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014. Released 07/05/2016 at 12:54 PM by PIO Joel Despain
