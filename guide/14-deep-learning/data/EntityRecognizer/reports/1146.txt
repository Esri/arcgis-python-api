Madison Police Department officers responded to the Hilldale Target on 01/17/18 at approximately 8:33 PM for a male inside the store who was causing a disturbance (knocking items over and yelling loudly).  Security officers at Target asked the person to leave and he then threatened to shoot one or both of the security officers. Numerous Target employees were very fearful for everyone's safety based on the suspect's behavior and threats. Officers arrested Benjamin Blunt on two counts of Disorderly Conduct. Benjamin Blunt was taken to the Dane County Jail.    Released 01/17/2018 at 11:43 PM by Lt. Gregory Esser
