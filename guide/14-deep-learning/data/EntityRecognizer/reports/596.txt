Two suspects described above tackled the victim who was walking home on N Thompson Dr.  Suspects removed the victim's wallet and ran while the victim chased them.  One suspect threatened to get a gun if the victim continued to chase them, and the 2nd suspect then returned the wallet to the victim, with the case missing.  Suspects then fled northbound on N Thompson Drive.
