 Officers were dispatched to the 4800 block of Hayes Rd. early this morning for a report of a man threatening another person with a handgun. When police arrived, the witness said the victim had left, but officers did locate the suspect after a search of the area. He was in possession of a handgun and arrested for carrying a concealed weapon. Released 02/15/2018 at 12:05 PM by PIO Joel Despain
