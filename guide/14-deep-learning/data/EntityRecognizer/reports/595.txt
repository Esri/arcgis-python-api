At 04:40 AM, MPD was dispatched to the intersection of Bridge Rd and Lake Point Dr for a report of shots fired. Officers located a victim who was operating his motor vehicle and had been speaking to another person in a different vehicle. The vehicles were approached by the suspect who for reasons unknown, fired shots at the vehicle striking it. There were no reported injuries. Investigation continues.   Released 03/05/2016 at 7:49 AM by Sgt. Eric Tripke
