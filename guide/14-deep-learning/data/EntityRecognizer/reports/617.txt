On 12-22-18, Madison officers were dispatched to the Mcdonald's restaurant at 1102 Regent street for a strong armed robbery. Two suspects covering their face, came in and ran to the back office, grabbed some money, and then ran out of the restaurant. There were no injuries to anyone and no weapon was displayed. The suspects fled in a vehicle and neither the suspects or the vehicle was located. Released 12/23/2018 at 1:08 AM by Sgt. Ryan Gibson
