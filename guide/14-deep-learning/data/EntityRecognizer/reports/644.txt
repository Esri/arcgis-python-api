 The MPD is seeking the public's help in identifying a man who was involved in a downtown disturbance last month. The suspect was among several who were part of a physical confrontation that took place in the 400 block of W. Gilman St. around 12:30 a.m. on February 25th. Anyone with information can contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014. Released 03/13/2017 at 1:15 PM by PIO Joel Despain
