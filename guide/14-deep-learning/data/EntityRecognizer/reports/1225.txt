 A City of Madison Parks' employee discovered a body this afternoon near the shoreline of Marshall Park, 2101 Allen Blvd. The park is in the City of Madison but adjoins Middleton. The MPD, Middleton Police and the Dane County Sheriff's Office are working with the Dane County Medical Examiner on the case. It will be up to the Medical Examiner to release the name of the deceased as well as the cause and manner of death. Released 04/20/2017 at 2:33 PM by PIO Joel Despain
