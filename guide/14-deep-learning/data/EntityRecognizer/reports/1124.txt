Subject entered the Subway at 1401 University Avenue, displaying a handgun and demanding the money in the till.  The subject took the money and fled towards Johnson St.  MPD responded to another subway store a short time later for a male believed to be matching the description of the armed robber.  That individual was contacted, and was deemed to not be involved.  Investigation is continuing. Released 10/16/2017 at 10:26 PM by Lt. Timothy Radke
