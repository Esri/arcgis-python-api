Officers were dispatched to a possible enticement in the 500 block of Zor Shrine Place.  Officers learned that a 14 year old, was walking in the area when approached by the suspect. The suspect told the victim to come over to him. The victim quickly walked away and took refuge in a nearby business.  The suspect followed the victim into the business and then loitered in the area for some time, before leaving on foot.  The suspect is described above.  Anyone with further information is asked to call the non-emergency dispatch number at 255-2345.
