An adult female pedestrian was crossing S Park St north of Hughes Pl, not using the crosswalk.  A vehicle was southbound and struck the pedestrian who was wearing all dark clothing and was in the roadway. The operator of the vehicle that struck the pedestrian immediately stopped and rendered aid along with other citizens in the area.  The pedestrian was transported to a local hospital with life threatening injuries. S Park St was closed for approximately 1 hour during scene processing.  The operator of the vehicle will not be cited.  Released 03/16/2018 at 7:55 AM by Lt. Jason Ostrenga
