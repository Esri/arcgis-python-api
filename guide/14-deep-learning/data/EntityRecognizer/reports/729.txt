Officers were sent to the area of S Park St and Drake St for a report of a disturbance possibly involving someone with a gun.  Several callers reported hearing, what sounded like, a large disturbance on Drake St near S Park St.  One caller reported hearing someone state "put down the gun".  Another caller stated a subject had a gun but later stated he only heard what he believed to be someone "dry firing" the gun, or pulling the trigger with no rounds being fired.  All participants left prior to police arrive and no reports of injuries we received related to this incident. Released 10/19/2016 at 2:57 AM by Lt. Anthony Fiore
