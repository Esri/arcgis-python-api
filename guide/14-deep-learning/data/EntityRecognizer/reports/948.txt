Madison Police responded to the intersection of W Wingra Drive at South Park St for a two vehicle crash with injuries.  During the course of the investigation, the operator of the vehicle traveling east on W Wingra Drive was identified as Todd W Schultz, W/M, 50 years old.  Schultz traveled through the red traffic signal on W Wingra Drive, and struck a vehicle traveling north on S Park St, operated by a pregnant 28 year old F/W.  Schultz admitted to consuming alcoholic beverages today, and refused to perform field sobriety testing. He was arrested for 3rd offense OMVWI. The female was transported to a local hospital for treatment related to minor injuries.  Released 12/16/2017 at 8:31 PM by Sgt. Nicholas Ellis
