Officers were dispatched to a serious car versus pedestrian crash in the 1800 block of S. Park St. A woman was struck by a vehicle as she walked across northbound S. Park St.  The woman who was struck suffered serious injuries.  Both directions of S. Park St. were shut down from 4:00 PM until 4:22 PM when the southbound lanes were opened.  The northbound lanes were opened at 5:18 PM.  The driver of the vehicle is cooperating with the ongoing investigation. Released 08/18/2016 at 7:31 PM by Sgt. Jamar Gary
