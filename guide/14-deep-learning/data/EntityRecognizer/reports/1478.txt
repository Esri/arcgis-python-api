 MPD officers, called by Capitol Police for backup late this morning, set up a perimeter outside the Hill Farms State Transportation Building, 4802 Sheboygan Ave., after a person of interest from an area robbery was spotted inside the facility. The 52 year-old-man was taken into custody without incident as he left the building. Sauk Prairie Police had asked other jurisdictions to be on the lookout for the man following a June 22nd armed robbery of a Sauk City credit union. Released 06/29/2017 at 1:06 PM by PIO Joel Despain
