 Madison Police were called to an injury crash near the 4700 blk of East Washington Frontage Road where a vehicle lost control and hit a tree near the Perkins Restaurant.  The vehicle broke apart and the engine block landed in the parking lot.   All three people in the vehicle were injured in the crash and transported to an area hospital. Injuries are not believed to be life threatening at this time. Driver condition and excess speed appear to be contributing factors. The crash investigation is continuing.  Released 11/30/2016 at 3:44 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
