 Officers were called to the 700 block of East Washington Avenue at 02:42 for a strong armed robbery.  A 24 year old male reported that his cash/phone were taken during this robbery and officers may have had contact with the suspect earlier in the night.  It was learned the suspect vehicle had in fact been stopped earlier this evening during a traffic stop and was described as a white 4 door.  No injuries occurred.  MPD Officers were able to locate the suspect.  He was arrested and taken to the Dane County Jail.    Released 02/18/2018 at 5:15 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
