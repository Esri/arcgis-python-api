At 5:34 AM Officers were sent to the 5300 block of Groveland Terrace for a fight in progress. While enroute to the area, it was reported that shots had been fired. Initial investigation has revealed that three (3) males had been in an altercation during which two (2) of the males sustained non-life threatening gunshot wounds to their lower extremities. One suspect is in custody. The injured parties were conveyed to a local hospital for treatment. All three (3) individuals were known to one another and it is believed there is no risk to the public. Investigation continues. Released 11/23/2017 at 9:42 AM by Sgt. Eric Tripke
