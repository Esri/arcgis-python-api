Multiple callers to 911 stated they heard what they believed to be gunshots in this area.  Police discovered damage to more than one home and at least one vehicle in this area, along with mulitple shell casings.  At the time of this release, police are not aware of anyone injured as a result of this incident.  Investigation is continuing.
