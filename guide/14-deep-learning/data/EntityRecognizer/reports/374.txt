Officers were dispatched to the 10 block of Mt Vernon Ct for a bullet that entered a residence.  Officers report that the victim was lying down on the bed when a bullet entered the apartment and passed within 6 inches of the victim.  There was also a 3 year old child playing in the room.  No one was injured and no one was taken into custody.  The investigation is ongoing. Anyone with information is asked to call Madison Area Crimestoppers at 266-6014 or the Dispatch Center at 255-2345.  Released 07/18/2017 at 8:54 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
