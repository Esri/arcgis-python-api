 A 74-year-old bicyclist suffered serious injuries following a collision with a SUV Monday afternoon. The peddler was on the Capital City Trail, crossing Nob Hill Road, when the crash took place. A MPD Traffic Specialist is investigating. The injured cyclist remains hospitalized. The SUV driver, a 20-year-old Madison man, volunteered to also go to a hospital for a blood draw. Released 11/15/2016 at 9:25 AM by PIO Joel Despain
