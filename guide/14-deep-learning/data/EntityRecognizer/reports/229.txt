Caller in a nearby apartment stated he awoke to a loud crack, and upon inspection it appeared a bullet had gone through his wall.  Investigation revealed that a nearby resident had been field-stripping a handgun when it fired.  No injuries were discovered during this investigation.  Suspect was arrested for Endangering Safety by Use of a Dangerous Weapon.
