On 4-20-19 at approximately 4:43 am, Madison Police Officers were dispatched to several areas between  Odana Rd and as far south as Schroeder Rd area for what sounded like multiple shots being fired. The 911 center received over a dozen calls about this. Officers did not locate any evidence, and no reported injuries at this time. Released 04/20/2019 at 6:23 AM by Sgt. Ryan Gibson
