 A Mequon man, in Madison for the Badger game this weekend, was attacked early Sunday morning in the 500 block of State St. Witnesses said the suspect tackled the victim and then began punching the Mequon man in the head rendering him unconscious. Bystanders alerted a MPD officer, who was nearby on another call. The suspect, who struggled with police, was arrested. The victim was taken to a hospital with head injuries. Released 10/09/2018 at 9:08 AM by PIO Joel Despain
