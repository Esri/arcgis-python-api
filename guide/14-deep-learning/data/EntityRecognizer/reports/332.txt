Madison Police responded to a report of shots fired at a business on the corner of Odana/Gammon Rd.  A short time later, Officers were dispatched to UW Urgent Care on Mineral Point Rd for a male with a gunshot wound.  This person was taken to the hospital with injuries that do not appear to be life-threatening.  At this point it appears the people involved may know each other.  This remains an on-going investigation by the Madison Police Department.   Released 12/13/2015 at 5:05 PM by Sgt. Jamar Gary
