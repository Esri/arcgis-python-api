MPD Officers responded to a report of possible shots fired in the 400 block of Gilman Street.  Further investigation led to witnesses who stated they had observed a disturbance in this area, followed by a series of what sounded like gunshots.  A canvass of the area was completed and officers discovered approximately 5 shell casings in the area.  At this time police are not aware of anyone injured in this incident, nor have police located any property damage associated with this incident. Released 01/22/2017 at 3:54 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
