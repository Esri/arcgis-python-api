 On 06/07/18, members of the Violent Crime Unit, Burglary Crime Unit, Special Investigations Unit, Gang Unit, Dane County Narcotics Task Force, Community Policing Teams, Neighborhood Officers and the FBI all worked together in an attempt to apprehend violent offenders in our community. The Individuals were wanted on valid warrants, probable cause or needed to be contacted reference current investigations. This Suppression Operation yielded 8 arrests, one recovered stolen auto, 1 handgun and over 130g of Heroin. The Madison Police Department believes that along with prevention and intervention methods, suppression efforts such as these are an effective additional tool to help reduce violent crimes in our community.   Released 06/08/2018 at 1:42 PM by P.O. Howard Payne
