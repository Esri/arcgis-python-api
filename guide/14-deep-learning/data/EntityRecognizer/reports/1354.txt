 The Madison Police Department responded to the 200 block of Kennedy Heights for a weapons violation. There was a disturbance resulting in multiple gun shots being fired. The suspect(s) fled the scene prior to MPD arrival. There were no injuries reported and the investigation is ongoing. If anyone has knowledge of the incident they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.   Released 05/13/2019 at 3:36 AM by Lt. Reginald Patterson
