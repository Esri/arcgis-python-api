Officers were dispatched to this area reference a stabbing.  Investigation is continuing, but at this point it appears that the male victim and female suspect have known each other for years, and during a disturbance between the two the male was stabbed in the chest.  Male victim has been transported to an area hospital for evaluation and medical treatment.  The suspect is currently at large. Released 01/19/2017 at 2:20 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
