 Madison Police responded to an attempted armed robbery at Rosati's pizza where two subjects entered the business and demanded money.  One suspect was armed with what appeared to be an "assault rifle" during incident.  The subjects ran from business without getting any money during this robbery attempt.  No injuries were reported and no money was taken during this incident. The same business was robbed on 4/18/17 under 2017-128345.   This investigation is continuing.  If anyone has information about this robbery please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers with information.   Released 04/23/2017 at 4:40 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
