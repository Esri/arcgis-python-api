 A 24 year old Madison man was arrested overnight on tentative charges of retail theft, carrying a concealed weapon and bail jumping. Cody M Veith was arrested at the Hy-Vee grocery store in the 3800 block of East Washington Avenue.  Veith had been detained on suspicion of retail theft.  A semi-automatic handgun was found in Veith's backpack.  The weapon was not used in the retail theft.  Veith was conveyed to the Dane County Jail.   .  Released 06/18/2018 at 11:50 AM by P.O. David Dexheimer
