The victim was walking to a friend's residence on Britta Pkwy when a black Dodge Charger approached him and 3 suspects got out and confronted him.  One suspect displayed a black semi-auto handgun.  They pushed him against the car and went through his pockets, taking an undisclosed amount of money.  Victim was not struck by the suspects but may have aggravated a pre-existing injury during the incident that is not life-threatening. Released 05/02/2016 at 3:47 AM by Lt. John Radovan
