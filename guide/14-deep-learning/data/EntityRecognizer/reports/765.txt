A female was approached by Corey Brownlee behind the BP gas station and after a few words, was threatened by Brownlee with a handgun.  The woman went into the BP to call police.  An officer, who was nearby, spotted Brownlee as he left the gas station.  Brownlee initially fled on foot, but was located a short distance away.  With Brownlee was a William Jones.  During the investigation, it was learned that Jones had entered the BP shortly before Brownlee.  Jones at that time had been wearing a mask.  Brownlee was arrested for second degree recklessly endangering safety and possession of THC with intent to distribute.  Jones was arrested for a probation violation.
