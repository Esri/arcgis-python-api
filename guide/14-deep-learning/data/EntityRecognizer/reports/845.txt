Officers were dispatched to the area of 2164 W Beltline Highway for a report of people shooting at each other.  Officers arrived on scene and located one male victim, who was shot. The victim was transported to a local hospital with non-life threatening injuries.  At this time there are no other injuries reported and no property damage.  There is no suspect information at this time.  Investigators and detectives are on scene and more information will be provided as it becomes available. Released 02/02/2017 at 8:46 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
