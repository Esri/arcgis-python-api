Madison and Monona Police have responded to reports of shots fired near the intersection of East Broadway and S. Stoughton Rd. Witnesses report seeing a gunman firing bullets at another vehicle. Initial reports suggest 10 to 12 rounds were fired and multiple shell casings have been recovered from the roadway. There is no confirmation that anyone has been shot, but this is a very fluid situation. Motorists should be advised that there could be traffic delays in the area as investigators do their jobs. Released 06/23/2017 at 2:53 PM by PIO Joel Despain
