 The Madison Police Department was dispatched to the Stop N Go gas station located at 4624 Monona Drive.  A female suspect entered the Stop N Go with a handgun and robbed the gas station of money and additional items.  The suspect fled in an unknown direction. If anyone has any knowledge of the incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.  Released 01/06/2019 at 11:38 PM by Lt. Reginald Patterson
