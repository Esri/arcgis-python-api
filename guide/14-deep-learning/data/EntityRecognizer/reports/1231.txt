Madison Police are investigating a car vs motorcycle crash at the intersection of Cottage Grove Rd and Acewood Blvd.  The motorcyclist is a 20 year old male from the greater Madison area.  He was transported to a local hospital but did not survive his injuries.    The intersection was closed while officers investigated the crash.  No further information will be released, pending notification of family  Released 05/07/2017 at 5:22 PM by Sgt. Joseph Engler
