Officers were dispatched to reports of shots fired in this area with vehicles fleeing the scene.  Responding Officers located two shell casings in a parking lot.  Investigation suggests that a fight had occurred, at which point someone brandished a handgun and fired it as a vehicle fled the scene.  A damaged vehicle was located nearby, but upon further investigation the damage does not appear to be consistent with these shots being fired.  As of this release, MPD is not aware of anyone being struck by gun shots during this incident.  Investigation is continuing. Released 07/09/2017 at 3:47 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
