On Friday June 22, 2018 at 5:06 am MPD officers were dispatched to the 2000 block of Leland Drive regarding an injured person. A 32 year old Madison man is receiving treatment at a local hospital for his injuries.  He was injured as a result of a dispute with another man. The identity of the suspect in this incident was known at the outset. He has not yet been contacted.  MPD personnel are actively attempting to contact him at his home. There is no known danger to the general public at this time. This information is preliminary and the incident remains quite active. Updates will be provided as they are available.  Released 06/22/2018 at 8:57 AM by P.O. David Dexheimer
