 The MPD was called to assist Madison Fire Department paramedics Monday night at Aldo Leopold Park, 2906 Traceway Dr., after a firework went off in a teenager's hand. EMS members told police the 18-year-old had lost parts of three fingers and suffered chest burns. At the hospital, the victim told an officer he had found the firework in the park and lit the fuse. It was not immediately clear what type of firework he ignited. Released 07/05/2017 at 8:44 AM by PIO Joel Despain
