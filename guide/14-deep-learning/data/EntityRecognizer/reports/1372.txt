On 12/18/15, the Madison Police Department received numerous reports of car windows being smashed out in areas of the south side.  Upon further investigation it appears the damage to the windows was caused as a result of them being shot out with a BB gun.  No injuries have been reported as a result of these incidents. If you have any information related to these windows being damaged, please call the Madison Police Department at 266-4275 or Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014. Released 12/19/2015 at 3:41 AM by Lt. Lori Chalecki
