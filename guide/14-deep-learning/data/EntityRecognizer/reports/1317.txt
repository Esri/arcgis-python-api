Officers were dispatched to the 10 block of E. Gorham St. at 8:38 p.m. on Wednesday night after a person was overheard yelling, "he put a gun to my head!" Officers found a person with a blunt force injury to his forehead, a bullet casing on the counter in an apartment and a bullet hole in the ceiling of the apartment. At least two persons left the scene prior to officers arriving. The Madison Police Violent Crime Detectives are continuing to follow up on this incident and ask that any witnesses or persons with information about this incident please contact the Violent Crime Unit at 608-267-8824 or persons wishing to remain anonymous may contact the Madison Area Crime Stoppers line at 608-266-6014.  Released 04/13/2017 at 12:44 PM by P.O. Kimberly Alan
