Police reponded to a residence in the 4900 block of Ilene Ln on the City's north side for a report of a 77 y.o. with advanced Alzheimer's that had left the residence.  It was unknown how long the person had been missing however at approximatley 1:55 AM officers were able to locate him a few blocks away.  The subject was checked out by MFD paramedics and returned home. Released 10/19/2016 at 4:57 AM by Lt. Anthony Fiore
