This morning, detectives from the MPD's Special Victims Unit (SVU) arrested a 42-year-old Madison man on a tentative charge of possession of child pornography. Ryan C. Bestul, age 42, was taken into custody after a search warrant was executed on his Rockwell Dr. home. MPD SWAT and agents from the Wisconsin's Department of Criminal Investigations (DCI) assisted SVU. SVU was acting on a "cybertip" received from DCI and the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children. Released 10/23/2018 at 12:48 PM by PIO Joel Despain
