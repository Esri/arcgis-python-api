 Officers were called to a hospital early this morning after a 45-year-old Fitchburg man checked himself in with a stab wound to his torso. The victim said he was walking on Fish Hatchery Rd. when two robbers approached from behind. During a struggle, he claims he was stabbed and that his wallet was stolen. He next walked home, and later decided to have medical staff look at the wound. It required a couple of stitches. He was not able to provide descriptive details of the robbers or the weapon. Released 07/02/2018 at 11:03 AM by PIO Joel Despain
