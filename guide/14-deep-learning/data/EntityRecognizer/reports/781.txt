Madison Police responded to the report of a crash involving a pedestrian on the Eastbound lanes of Hwy 12/18.  Officers arrived and have shut down the belt line for the foreseeable future due to the traffic investigation. Madison Police would like to urge motorist to find alternate routes of travel until we have opened the roadway to traffic later this morning.    Released 11/21/2016 at 3:32 AM by Sgt. Shannon Blackamore
