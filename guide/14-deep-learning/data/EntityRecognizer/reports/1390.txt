 A 22-year-old Madison man showed up at a hospital Tuesday night around 8:00 p.m. with a hand cut. He said he got the wound while struggling with a knife wielding man during a mugging several hours earlier. The victim claims he was robbed of his wallet after two men – including the one with the knife - pulled him into a garage on Kanazawa Circle around 4:40 p.m. Released 03/08/2017 at 9:57 AM by PIO Joel Despain
