Madison Police were in the area of Pilgrim Rd., in search of suspects that fled from a vehicle which contained a gun and other contraband.  The officers encountered Paul Jackson Jr., near a City Park and discovered a loaded .22 caliber gun on him.  The gun had been reported as stolen in a prior incident.  The weapon recovered from Paul Jackson Jr., was the second gun recovered by officers on this same date in this area.  Paul Jackson was arrested and conveyed to the Dane County Jail.    Released 08/29/2016 at 3:52 AM by Sgt. Shannon Blackamore
