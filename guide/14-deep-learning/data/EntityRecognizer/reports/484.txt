 Officers recovered many shell casings in the street after several callers reported hearing gunfire in the 200 block of S. Fair Oaks Ave. early Saturday morning. One resident said, after being awakened by the reports, he looked out to see a car racing north on S. Fair Oaks. Police found no property damage or injured people.
