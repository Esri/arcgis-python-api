A 34-year-old Town of Madison man, accused of peddling crack cocaine, was arrested in the 2300 block of Badger Parkway Friday afternoon following a Dane County Narcotics Task Force investigation. Its leaders indicate the suspect is a confirmed gang member with a lengthy criminal history that includes convictions for weapons, cocaine and heroin. He was taken into custody, without incident, outside of an apartment building.  Released 01/26/2016 at 11:43 AM by PIO Joel Despain
