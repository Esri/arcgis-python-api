The City of Madison Police Department took a stolen auto report from the West Police District at 4:42 PM today.  The vehicle owner came home to find his garage door kicked in and the car taken.  At approximately 6:25 PM, the vehicle owner saw his stolen vehicle being driven in Woodman's parking lot on S Gammon Road.  Madison Police were able to stop the vehicle on east bound US HY 12 near John Nolen Drive. The driver, 15 year old Black Male, and the passenger, 13 year old Hispanic Female, were taken into custody.  The vehicle was returned to the owner. Released 04/07/2016 at 9:41 PM by Sgt. Nicholas Ellis
