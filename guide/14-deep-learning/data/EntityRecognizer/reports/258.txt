An MPD officer was waved down by a witness that reported a disturbance underneath the overhang of the Memorial Library.  The suspect knocked over a meal and the male victim demanded an apology.  The suspect grabbed the female victim by the neck and made threats to the male.  Witnesses reported that the suspect was in possession of an unknown object but nothing was found on the suspect.  The suspect was arrested for Disorderly Conduct.  There were no injuries reported. Released 07/30/2018 at 7:28 PM by Lt. Jamar Gary
