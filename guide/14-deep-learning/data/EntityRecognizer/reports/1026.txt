 Members of the Dane County Narcotics Task Force developed information that a Madison man was peddling cocaine and heroin and targeting sales to known addicts at a local rehabilitation facility. Members of the Task Force pulled the suspect over in the Badger Road area Monday morning and took him into custody on several tentative charges including possession with intent to deliver cocaine. Released 03/12/2019 at 12:23 PM by PIO Joel Despain
