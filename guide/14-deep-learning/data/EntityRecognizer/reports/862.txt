 Members of the MPD's Violent Crimes Unit, along with other departmental resources, are on the scene of a shooting at this hour. Witnesses report multiple shots being fired in the 2700 block of McDivitt Rd., with one person, a male, being hit by the gunfire. The preliminary investigation indicates a bullet may have also struck a nearby residence. More information will be released as it becomes available. Released 09/28/2016 at 10:13 AM by PIO Joel Despain
