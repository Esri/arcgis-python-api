A pizza delivery man responds to an address in the 6300 block of Hammersly Rd.  He is told by the resident that he didn't order the pizza.  As the driver is walking back to his car, he is attacked by the two suspects.  The suspects battered the delivery driver and stole the pizza and some cash.  The suspects then fled on foot.
