 A robbery victim showed up at the North Police District station this morning to report a crime that took place last week. The 48-year-old said he was inside his car, parked in a lot in the 1500 block of Troy Dr., when a gunman approached his window and held him up on the evening of May 14th. The criminal took the victim's cash and fled. Released 05/20/2019 at 12:51 PM by PIO Joel Despain
