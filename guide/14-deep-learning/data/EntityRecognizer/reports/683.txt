Shortly after midnight on 6/5/18 the Madison Police Dept. was dispatched to an attempted armed robbery at the Greenbush Bakery in the 1300 block of Regent St.  While the male employee was closing up shop the suspect entered and demanded money from the register.  The employee fled from the suspect and the suspect left the store without any cash.  The suspect was described as either a Hispanic male or mixed race male who was approximately 5'8" tall with a thin build and was wearing dark colored clothing.  MPD's investigation into this attempted armed robbery is ongoing. Released 06/05/2018 at 1:48 AM by Sgt. Kurt Wege
