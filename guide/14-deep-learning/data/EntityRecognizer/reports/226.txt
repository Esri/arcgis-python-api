Officers were sent to the BP Gas Station reference multiple shots being fired.  When officers arrived on scene, multiple vehicles were fleeing the area.  Officers did locate one victim on scene with a non-life threatening injury.  Two other victims arrived on their own at Meriter Hospital, also with non-life threatening injuries.  A fourth victim arrived at UW Sun Prairie.  The extent of this victim's injuries were unknown, but it did not appear to be life threatening.  The investigation is on-going at this time. Released 02/19/2017 at 4:53 AM by Sgt. Galen Wiering
