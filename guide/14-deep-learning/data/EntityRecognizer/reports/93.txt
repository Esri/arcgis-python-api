 West District officers and the MPD's Violent Crime Unit are investigating an attempted homicide this morning following domestic violence inside a Chester Dr. home. Officers were called there shortly before 1:30 a.m. and found a 25-year-old woman with multiple stab wounds. She was taken to a hospital where she is listed in stable condition, and is expected to survive. There were several small children in the home at the time of the attack. The suspect, a 37-year-old man, fled the scene in a car and is believed to have left the Madison area. He remains at large. Released 03/15/2018 at 9:28 AM by PIO Joel Despain
