Officers were sent to the area of 3100 Webb Ave. reference a report of shots being fired.  Callers to dispatch provided further information that one person was shot and was being driven to the hospital.  Officers were able to locate the victim and followed them to a local hosptial.  The victim is being treated for a non-life threatening wound.  The investigation is still on-going at this time.  Released 08/08/2017 at 10:21 PM by Sgt.
