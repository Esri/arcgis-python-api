Officers were dispatched to an apartment building in the 2200 block of Cypress Way reference an unconscious man.  Upon arrival to the scene the man was already in his apartment.  Officers heard sounds from inside the apartment that indicated that the subject needed his welfare checked.  MFD and MPD made entry and were met with the man pointing a fiream toward them.  Officers successfully de-escalated the situation and took the man into custody without incident.  There were no injuries. Released 02/10/2017 at 10:49 PM by Sgt. Jamar Gary
