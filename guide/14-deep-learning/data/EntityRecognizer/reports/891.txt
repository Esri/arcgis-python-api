MPD officers were dispatched to the Subway in reference to an attempted robbery.  Witnesses reported that the suspect walked into the restaurant with his hoodie over his face.  The suspect demanded money from the cash register but the clerk did not comply.  The suspect then fled on foot.  Nothing was stolen and there were no reported injuries. Anyone who has information about this incident should contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers. Released 07/19/2018 at 9:04 PM by Lt. Jamar Gary
