On December 6th, 2018, at approximately 7:45 a.m., a juvenile female was walking to a bus stop in the 700 block of Odana Lane when she was grabbed by an adult, white male.  The child was able to get away from the man.  The suspect had scratches on his face and white facial hair.  He had a mustache and a goatee.  The suspect was wearing a green and black jacket, a blue baseball cap and black, circular eyeglasses.  Anyone with information should call Crimestoppers or the Madison Police Department.  Released 12/06/2018 at 6:43 PM by Sgt.
