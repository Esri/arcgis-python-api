Officers responded to an alarm at Dick's Sporting Goods, 237 West Towne Mall.  Upon arrival, Officers observed glass breakage and then a male running from inside of the store.  Officers ran after this individual and were able to catch him and take him into custody.  Another suspect seen running from the store was not located or arrested.  The above suspect was carrying a backpack, later determined to contain approximately 13 airsoft and pellet guns, which appeared to have been the targeted merchandise from the burglary.  This investigation is continuing. Released 04/27/2017 at 3:37 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
