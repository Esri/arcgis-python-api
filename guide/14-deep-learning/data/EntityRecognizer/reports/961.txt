 Madison Police responded to an armed robbery at 4222 East Washington Ave (BP) at 22:28. Two black females entered the store and robbed the clerk at gun point.  The suspects left on foot and a canine track ended about 1 block away. An undetermined amount of cash was taken from the register, cigarettes from display in store and cell phone of clerk were taken in this robbery.   Officers are checking local hotels and video is being obtained.  Investigation continuing. Released 12/20/2017 at 2:44 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
