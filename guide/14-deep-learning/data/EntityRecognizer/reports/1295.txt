 A Madison cab driver said he was punched multiple times after he kicked a would-be passenger out of his taxi early Saturday morning. The victim said the suspect entered his cab in the 200 block of State St. and made a derogatory statement to him. The driver told the man to get out. After exiting, the victim says the suspect came around and opened his door and started delivering punches. Passersby helped police locate the suspect, who was still in the area. The driver was not seriously injured. Released 01/07/2019 at 12:09 PM by PIO Joel Despain
