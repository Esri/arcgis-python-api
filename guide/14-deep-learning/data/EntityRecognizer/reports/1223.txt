On 02/13/2018 at approximately 10:59 PM, Madison officers were called to the 5200 block of Piccadilly Drive for a report of a disturbance and a stabbing.  Officers arrived and learned that a 19-year-old Madison resident stabbed a family-associate, in the back/shoulder area, after a family disturbance. The 53-year-old victim was taken to a local hospital, and was treated for non-life threatening injuries as a result of being stabbed. The 19-year-old suspect was taken to the Dane County Jail on tentative charges of 2nd Degree Reckless Endangering Safety, Domestic Criminal Damage to Property, and Domestic Disorderly Conduct. The victim was treated and released from the hospital, but was also taken to the Dane County Jail on an outstanding warrant. Released 02/14/2018 at 3:48 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
