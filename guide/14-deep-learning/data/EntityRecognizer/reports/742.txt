At approximately 5:46 pm, officers were dispatched to the area of East Washington Ave near First Street for a motor vehicle versus pedestrian accident. When officers arrived, the driver was no longer on scene and a twelve year old girl had been struck by the vehicle. The girl was taken to a local hospital with serious but non life threatening injuries. The driver did later come to a Madison Police station to speak to officers. From the investigation, the driver was charged with hit and run causing injury.  Released 12/28/2018 at 1:56 AM by Sgt. Ryan Gibson
