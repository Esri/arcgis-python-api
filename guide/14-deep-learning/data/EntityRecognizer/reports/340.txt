Multiple callers to 911 reported hearing what they believed to be between 5 and 7 gunshots in this area.  Responding officers were able to locate what appeared to be a bullet hole in a plastic bin near a residence in this area, along with one spent shell casing.  No further damage located and no one reported injured at this time. Released 02/14/2017 at 4:46 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
