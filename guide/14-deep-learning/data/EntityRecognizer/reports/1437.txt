The Madison Police Department was called to the 200 block of W. Lakelawn Place reference two men fighting. The caller further said one had a gun and was chasing the other man towards the UW-Madison Memorial Union. Upon investigating the incident, it was determined that the two men were not actually fighting, but most likely were acquaintances that ran from the area together.  It was not confirmed that either had a handgun.  It is not believed that the public is in any danger reference these individuals.  One man was described as a black male wearing a Texas A&M jersey and the other was described as a black male wearing a long black leather jacket.  If anyone has information on who these individuals might be, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department of the Madison Area Crimestoppers program.  Released 10/04/2018 at 12:37 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
