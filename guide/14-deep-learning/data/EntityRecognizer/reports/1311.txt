 A drunken driver was speeding on Milwaukee St. early this morning when he lost control, drove into oncoming traffic lanes, and barreled into two parked vehicles. They ended up on a front lawn. All cars were significantly damaged. The intoxicated driver's was smoking and in the roadway. The front compartment had been pushed into the backseat. Neighborhood residents could hear the man yelling for help, and they were able pull him out of a rear window. The passersby cared for the man until police arrived. He was taken to a hospital with non-life-threatening injuries and was processed for fourth offense drunken driving. Released 12/19/2018 at 9:26 AM by PIO Joel Despain
