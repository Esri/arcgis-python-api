A 22 year old Madison man said that he pulled over to use his telephone, in the 900 block of Sequoia Trail.  While stopped, the victim reports that the suspect approached him and asked if he could use his telephone.  During this contact, the suspect displayed a knife and there was a struggle and the victim was cut on the hand.  He was taken to a hospital for treatment of minor injuries.  A witness called 911 and stayed with the victim until first responders arrived.  The suspect was last seen running from the scene.  The suspect was described as a 15-16 year old male black, with dreadlocks, wearing an orange sweatshirt and black jeans.  The investigation is continuing. Released 03/22/2016 at 7:03 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
