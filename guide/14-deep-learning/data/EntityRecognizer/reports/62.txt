 The MPD found a suspected gunman hiding in a chest freezer around 8:00 p.m. last night. Officers had been searching a Parker Place apartment building basement when they found Demetrius C. Murray, age 31, Madison. They were looking to arrest him after he allegedly fired a handgun during a struggle with a woman early yesterday morning. The victim, who is acquainted with the suspect, said he came to her Magnolia Lane apartment around 3:00 a.m. They got into an argument and he pulled the weapon when she tried to get him to leave. The two ended up in a physical struggle, and the gun discharged. The woman said the suspect fled after a bullet hit a wall. There were no reported injuries.
