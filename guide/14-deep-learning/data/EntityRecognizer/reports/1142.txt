Several callers reported hearing a crash in the 200 block of Swanton Rd and a dark colored SUV leaving the scene.  Officers arrived on scene to find a total of 3 parked vehicle struck by the suspect vehicle.  Officers were able to follow the trail of leaking fluid from the suspect vehicle for several blocks and found Morales-Lopez with his badly damaged vehicle.  Morales-Lopez arrested for OMVWI and Hit & Run. Released 10/19/2016 at 5:08 AM by Lt. Anthony Fiore
