Several callers reported hearing a gunshot from the area of 100 block Kennedy Heights.  Upon arrival officers were able to locate a single bullet casing consistent with witness statements as to where the gunfire occurred.  Witnesses stated three men were arguing in the parking lot.  One of the subjects begins to leave in a vehicle at which point the suspect fires one shot in the general direction of the vehicle. No injuries, victims or property damage located at this time. Released 11/08/2016 at 12:24 AM by Lt. Anthony Fiore
