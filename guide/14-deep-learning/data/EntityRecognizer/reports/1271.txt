 Officers were called to a burglary alarm early this morning after movement was detected in the teachers' lounge at Orchard Ridge Elementary School. Two teens were found hiding in the library. They refused to answer officers' questions. An outside door was found to have been pried open with a shovel. Both teens were taken to the Juvenile Reception Center on tentative burglary charges.  Released 05/02/2018 at 11:33 AM by PIO Joel Despain
