 MPD SWAT assisted the Dane County Narcotics Task Force in serving a search warrant Wednesday morning at the Grand Stay Hotel, 5317 High Crossing Blvd. Task Force has been conducting an investigation into cocaine dealing and the alleged peddler has been staying in one of the hotel's rooms. He was found there and arrested without incident. Cocaine, marijuana and cash were among items seized. Released 01/17/2019 at 12:38 PM by PIO Joel Despain
