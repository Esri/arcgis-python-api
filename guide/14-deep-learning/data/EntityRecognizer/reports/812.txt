The MPD found eight bullet casings near the intersection of Raymond Rd. and Marty Rd. Tuesday. A concerned citizen told investigating officers that he heard possible gunfire in the area around 3:55 a.m. There were no immediate reports of injuries or property damage. The incident was reported to the MPD several hours after the reports. Released 04/20/2016 at 10:56 AM by PIO Joel Despain
