MPD responded to a report of 10-20 people fighting in the street. Upon arrival, officers located a male victim who had sustained knife wounds to his head and facial areas. Officers report the suspect fled the area prior to police arrival. The victim was treated at a local hospital for non-life threatening wounds. It is known from the investigation that the victim and suspect know each other and  there is no threat to the public. Investigation Continues.... Released 04/01/2018 at 10:21 AM by Sgt. Eric Tripke
