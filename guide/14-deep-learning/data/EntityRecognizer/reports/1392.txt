 A 14-year-old student was injured late this morning after stepping into traffic near West High School. Witnesses said the victim walked out between cars parked on Highland Ave. and was knocked down by a slow-moving vehicle. The girl hit her head and may have been unconscious for a short time. When police arrived she was alert. She was taken to a hospital with a head cut. The driver, who stayed on scene, was not ticketed. Released 10/02/2017 at 1:59 PM by PIO Joel Despain
