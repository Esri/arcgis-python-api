Multiple callers report hearing 5-6 shots fired near the parking lot of Vision's, with people scattering and running away.  Police locate at least two holes, believed to be bullet holes, in the building and a vehicle hit as well, and recover at least 2 shell casings on scene.  Officers also later locate a man who suffered a non-life-threatening gun shot wound at an area hospital, believed to be associated with this incident.  Investigation is continuing. Released 05/24/2017 at 3:06 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
