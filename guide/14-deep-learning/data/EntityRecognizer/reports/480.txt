MPD Officers were dispatched to the 6900 block of Carnwood Road in the City of Madison regarding a motor vehicle crash.  The vehicle in question left the roadway and crashed into a single family home which was occupied by a family (including children).  The Fire Department as well as MG&E responded to ensure the gas was shut off.  The driver of the vehicle fled from the scene on foot but was arrested a short time later.  He was issued citations for OMVWI-1st Offense, Reckless Driving, and Hit and Run.  No one from inside the home was injured and the crash remains under investigation.     Released 05/03/2018 at 4:15 AM by Sgt. SGT Nathan Becker
