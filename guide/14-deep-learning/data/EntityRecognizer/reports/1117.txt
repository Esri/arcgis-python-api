Officers were dispatched to a business in the 2700 block of East Washington Avenue.  According to store employees, the suspect took a bag of chips and began to leave the store without paying for them.  An employee attempts to intercede when the suspect drew a knife and made stabbing motions towards the employee.  The suspect fled but was located by Officers in the area and was arrested.  Subject was transported to the PSB for Armed Robbery and Recklessly Endangering Safety. Released 08/22/2017 at 11:05 PM by Lt. Timothy Radke
