On Wednesday morning August 03, 2016 at about 5:07 a.m. it was discovered that the BP gas station at 6410 Millpond Rd had been burglarized.  Entry was gained to the building by smashing the front door window.  The target of the burglary appears to have been cigarettes although the exact loss has not yet been determined.  The incident remains under investigation.  Anyone with information can contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014 or send a tip online; http://madisonareacrimestoppers.org/Callers may remain anonymous and may be eligible for a cash reward.   Released 08/03/2016 at 1:40 PM by P.O. David Dexheimer
