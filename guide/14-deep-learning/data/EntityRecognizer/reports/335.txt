On 10/18/17 at 12:59 pm the victim reported to MPD that 3 suspects attempted to rob him.  The victim reported that he arranged with one of the suspects through social media to meet to exchange cellular phones.  Once the victim swapped his cell phone for the suspect's cell phone, the suspect then pulled a firearm on the victim.  The victim then jumped in his car and fled the area.  As the victim left the area, his vehicle was shot at.  The damage was consistent with a bb/pellet gun.  The victim was not injured and the suspects were not located.  The victim described the suspects as 3 male blacks, 20 to 25 years of age with one having his hair in braids.  The investigation is ongoing. Released 10/18/2017 at 8:19 PM by Sgt. Jamar Gary
