On 11/09/15, at 1820 hours, the Madison Police Department responded to an armed robbery at the BP Gas Station, 699 S Gammon Rd.  The female victim reported she had been sitting at the pumps texting.  The victim stated she had been sitting there about 10 minutes when her passenger door opened and the suspect displayed a gun and demanded "everything."  The victim turned over an undisclosed amount of cash, which the suspect took and then fled the area. No one was injured in this incident and there were no other victim's that the Madison Police Department is aware of at this time. Released 11/10/2015 at 2:19 AM by Lt. Lori Chalecki
