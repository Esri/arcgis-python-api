The MPD apprehended three of four car theft suspects this morning after Waunakee Police passed on information that a white Mercedes, stolen from their jurisdiction, was thought to be traveling in Madison. The car was spotted backed into a parking stall at McDonald's, 1102 Regent St. Numerous MPD squad cars and officers converged on the Mercedes. Two occupants ran. Officers tracked down one and took two other teens into custody who were still inside the car. Two of the arrested teens are connected to a group of young people who have been committing a series of recent area crimes to include car thefts. This is a Waunakee Police Department case. Released 11/20/2018 at 12:05 PM by PIO Joel Despain
