 A pizza delivery driver was robbed of his cash while making a delivery in the 800 block of Kottke Dr. last night. The 18-year-old victim said he was approached by a masked man while he was outside of a delivery location. The robber threatened the victim while keeping a hand in a pocket; however, no weapon was seen. The masked man fled on foot and the driver returned to his place of employment to report the crime. Released 06/03/2019 at 9:21 AM by PIO Joel Despain
