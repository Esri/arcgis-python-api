 There were multiple calls from concerned citizens in the area of Dwight Dr. and Independence Lane last night. All were certain they heard between five and nine gunshots. A 34-yar-old woman told officers she got down on the floor as reports rang out. Officers canvassed the neighborhood but did not find casings or damage. A witness reported seeing two young men running from the area shortly after the incident. Released 10/05/2018 at 9:33 AM by PIO Joel Despain
