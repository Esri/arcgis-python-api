 The MPD is looking to identify a man who has been making sexually inappropriate and derogatory comments to a woman who is a downtown bicycle commuter. She says the man also rides a bike and she has seen him numerous times in the area of Brittingham Park. The man's comments have made the victim feel violated and unsafe. The MPD would like to address this behavior. Anyone with information on the identity of the man is asked to contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014.
