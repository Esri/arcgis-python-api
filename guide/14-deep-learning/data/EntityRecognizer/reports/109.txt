On May 2nd 2019 at approximately 11:56 p.m. Madison Police Department responded to the 5000 block of Milwaukee St. for a weapons violation. Multiple gunshots were fired at a residence causing damage to the property. The occupants of the dwelling were not injured. If anyone has knowledge of the incident they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.  Released 05/03/2019 at 5:04 AM by Lt. Reginald Patterson
