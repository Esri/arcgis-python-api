A bank robber held up Bank Mutual, 5521 Odana Rd., in the past hour. He claimed to have a gun, although no weapon was actually seen. Tellers said the criminal stuttered the entire time he was speaking and appeared to be wearing a brown wig.  He fled with cash and the MPD is currently in the area searching for leads and clues. Anyone with information should contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014.  Released 01/10/2017 at 3:36 PM by PIO Joel Despain
