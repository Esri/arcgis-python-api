The Madison Police Department was called to the Walgreens at 2909 E. Washington Avenue today reference a robbery.  The suspect (see above for description) demanded cash and merchandise from the female employee.  The employee was not injured.  The suspect did not brandish a weapon. The suspect left and fled south west from the store.  He was given an undisclosed amount of cash and some merchandise.  If anyone has any information reference this incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program. Released 11/01/2018 at 11:58 PM by Lt. Daniel Nale
