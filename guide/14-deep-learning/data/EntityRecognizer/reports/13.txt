 A Turbot Drive resident was robbed Sunday after two armed men forced their way into his apartment. The victim said both intruders were carrying handguns. He was tied up and left in a bathroom while the men stole numerous items, including electronics and several pairs of expensive jeans. The victim was able to free himself and later contacted police. The preliminary investigation indicates he was specifically targeted by the criminals. Released 03/11/2019 at 11:18 AM by PIO Joel Despain
