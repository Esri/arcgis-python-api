The Madison Police Department has investigated a school threat that was sent Saturday via social media.  MPD Detectives were able to identify and contact the juvenile who posted the threat.  The juvenile was arrested for 2 counts of Unlawful Use of Computerized Communications Systems.  It has been determined that the school threat is not credible Released 06/03/2018 at 6:10 PM by Sgt. Stephanie Drescher
