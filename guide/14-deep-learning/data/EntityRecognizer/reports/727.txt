Officers observed Hatchett operating a vehicle on the eastside of Madison.  He was recognized as being involved in a previous armed robbery.  Officers attempted a traffic stop and Hatchett fled in the vehicle.  A short vehicle pursuit ensued.  After approximately a minute, Hatchett stopped the vehicle and fled on foot.  With the assistance of a K9, Hatchett was tracked and located hiding under a porch.  Hatchett was taken into custody without incident.  There was no damage and no one was injured during the pursuit or the arrest.  Hatchett was taken to the Dane County Jail on charges of armed robbery, eluding, second degree recklessly endangering safety, obstructing, and delivery of a controlled substance.
