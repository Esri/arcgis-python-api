The Madison Police Department responded to the area of East Mifflin Street and North Blount Street regarding a Weapons Offense (Shots Fired) after several people called 911 to report hearing multiple gunshots.  After arrival to the area, police contacted one man who believed he was targeted but was otherwise not injured.  Police did not find any property damage and there were no reported injuries.  One shell casing was recovered near the intersection of North Livingston Street and East Washington Avenue.  The investigation is ongoing at this time.  If anyone has any information regarding this incident, please contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crime Stoppers program.  Released 09/13/2018 at 4:55 AM by Sgt. SGT Nathan Becker
