 A woman visiting Madison from New York told police she was sexually assaulted early Sunday morning inside a car after getting a ride with a stranger. She said she had been socializing downtown, and that she was assaulted by a man who drove her back to where she was staying on Ohio Ave. Detectives are working with the victim to obtain more details.
