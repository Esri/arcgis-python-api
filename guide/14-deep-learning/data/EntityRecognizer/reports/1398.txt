 Recently Homeland Security and the U.S. Postal Service intercepted a package mailed to a 38-year-old Town of Madison woman. It contained ecstasy pills and powdered MDMA. The Dane County Narcotics Task Force was contacted and this past Tuesday a search warrant was executed on the suspect's Badger Lane apartment. In the course of the investigation, more than 600 ecstasy pills were seized as was 30 grams of powered MDMA. A loaded 9mm handgun was recovered. The suspect was arrested at her apartment without incident. Released 04/06/2018 at 1:07 PM by PIO Joel Despain
