This afternoon, a Madison man was shot inside the Capitol Petro Mart, 2702 E. Washington Ave. Officers were called to the scene around 2:20 p.m. and have detained the person who is believed to have shot the 34-year-old. Detectives from the Violent Crime Unit are currently trying to sort out what led to the gunfire. The injured man was taken to a hospital with what is believed to be a non life-threatening wound. At this hour, no one has been arrested.     Released 09/25/2017 at 3:23 PM by PIO Joel Despain
