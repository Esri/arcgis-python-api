 Two loaded handguns were seized from a Chicago area man following a routine traffic stop on Raymond Rd. Saturday afternoon. A MPD officer pulled a car over for speeding, and the weapons were later found in a passenger's backpack. The 18-year-old told police the guns were for protection. He was arrested for carrying a concealed weapon. Released 04/18/2017 at 9:34 AM by PIO Joel Despain
