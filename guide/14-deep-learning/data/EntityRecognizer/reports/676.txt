Officers were dispatched to a home invasion where an adult male resident had been shot. He was transported to a local hospital with what appeared to be a non-life threatening gunshot wound to his shoulder area. The suspects had fled the area and were not located. This is an active investigation. Anyone with information is asked to call the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014.  Released 01/24/2016 at 10:19 AM by Lt. Timothy Strassman
