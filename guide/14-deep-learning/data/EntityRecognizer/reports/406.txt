 Madison Police were called to a strong-armed robbery at the BP gas station in the 4200 blk of East Washington Avenue.  A suspect entered the store and demanded money before fleeing on foot with an undetermined amount of cash.  The suspect was described as a white male, 5'10", slender build and was last seen wearing a gray hoodie. If anyone has information about this crime please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crimestoppers.  Released 05/04/2016 at 2:45 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
