A motorist called for police after his car was hit with paintballs early this morning in the 4600 block of Hayes Rd. A responding officer noted five areas on the car that had been struck with blue paint. A car parked in the area was also found to have been hit with paintballs. It appeared someone had been standing near an apartment complex, firing a paintball gun at passing cars. No suspect was located. Released 06/23/2017 at 9:52 AM by PIO Joel Despain
