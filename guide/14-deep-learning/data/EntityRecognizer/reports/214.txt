 Several officers were sent to the 300 block of N. Frances St. early this morning following reports of people fighting in the street. The MPD learned one person, a 29-year-old Madison man, had been knocked unconscious after being "coldcocked" by another man who had fled the scene. The victim was transported to a hospital with a broken nose and other possible facial fractures. Released 09/05/2018 at 9:39 AM by PIO Joel Despain
