On 05/07/2018 at approximately 9:47 PM, Madison Police Department officers responded to an armed robbery that occurred at the Little Caesars - 700 S. Gammon Road. Two younger male suspects entered the business and one had a handgun. No one was injured as a result of the robbery. The suspects were described as being African American Males - late teens to early twenties. Both suspects fled the area on foot and took an undisclosed amount of U.S. currency. The investigation continues.  Released 05/08/2018 at 3:46 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
