Madison police responded to the report of a large gathering of people and "shots fired."  Upon arrival, officers noted that all parties had departed.  During a canvass of the area no victims nor damage were found; however, they did find a single gun casing/shell.  No arrests have been made.  Madison Police ask that anyone with information contact Madison Crime Stoppers at 266-6014.  Released 04/09/2016 at 2:05 AM by Sgt. Shannon Blackamore
