 Madison Police were called to the Kwik Trip in the 4400 block of East Buckeye Road at 00:33 for a customer who was robbed in the parking lot.  A 34 year old male was walking out of the store when he was approached by a male, who pushed the victim and grabbed his wallet from his pocket.  The victim then grabbed onto the suspect and they wrestled on the ground over the wallet.  The suspect got up and said he had a knife before running from the scene (with the wallet).   The suspects coat was at the scene and a knife was located on the ground which may have fallen out during struggle.  If you have any information about this incident please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers.  Released 08/25/2018 at 2:57 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
