 A Madison cab driver contacted the MPD early Sunday morning after being flagged down by an injured man near the corner of Raymond Rd. and Gilbert Rd. The 54-year-old Madison victim was found to have a several facial fractures, a broken hand, chipped teeth and a brain bleed. He indicated to MPD that he was out drinking with a couple of men, and believes they are responsible for his injuries, although it was not clear what exactly happened to him. Released 10/02/2017 at 11:09 AM by PIO Joel Despain
