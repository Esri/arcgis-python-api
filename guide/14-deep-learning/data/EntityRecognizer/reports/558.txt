On October 19, 2017 at approximately 1:15am Madison Police Officers were dispatched to the 200 block of State Street regarding a weapons offense.  Upon arrival, officers locate the victim and find he has sustained a non-life threatening stab wound to his left torso area.  The victim was conveyed to a local hospital where he was interviewed but is not cooperating with the investigation.  The immediate area was thoroughly canvassed but the scene of the crime has not been located and a suspect has not been identified.   Released 10/19/2017 at 2:33 AM by Sgt. Nathan Becker
