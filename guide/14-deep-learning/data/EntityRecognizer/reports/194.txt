A disturbance occurred on the 300 Bl Swanton Rd..  The person involved in that disturbance got into a vehicle with two others and drove away.  The other person involved in that disturbance followed in a vehicle.  Another person in the first vehicle fired rounds from a handgun at the following vehicle.  We are not aware of any damages or injuries from those shots fired.  The vehicles then crashed on Hamlet Pl..  Two of the people in the first vehicle fled and were not located after a K9 track.  A handgun and shell casing was recovered.  At this time no arrests have been made but the investigation is continuing.  Released 09/06/2016 at 2:59 AM by Lt. John Radovan
