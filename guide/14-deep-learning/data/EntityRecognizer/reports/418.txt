Vance Peronto was in Hyvee at 3801 E Washington Ave when he saw an unattended wallet in a purse.  He took the wallet and tried to walk away from it.  When the wallet's owner confronted Peronto he shoved her and fled on foot.  He was chased down by Hyvee lost prevention and held until MPD arrived.  He was arrested without incident and is at the Dane County PSB.  The victim was not injured.  Released 09/10/2016 at 2:54 AM by Lt.
