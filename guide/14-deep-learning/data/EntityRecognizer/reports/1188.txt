 The Dane County Narcotics Task Force seized three handguns, including one stolen in a Fitchburg burglary, while arresting a suspected heroin dealer during the execution of a search warrant on Sycamore Ave. Tuesday morning. MPD SWAT and K9 Krahnie helped DCNTF clear the residence without incident. Three cell phones, drug paraphernalia and documents were among items also collected by the law enforcement team. Identifying and arresting heroin dealers remains a top priority for DCNTF.  Released 08/19/2016 at 9:10 AM by PIO Joel Despain
