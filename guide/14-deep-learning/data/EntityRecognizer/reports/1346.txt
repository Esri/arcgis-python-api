On 02/22/2018 at approximately 1:22 AM Madison officers were sent to the area outside of 17 N Wickham Court to investigate a report of an armed robbery. Officers learned that victims were confronted by an unknown male suspect who had a handgun and demanded money.  None of the victims were physically injured during this robbery.  The suspect was described as being a 17-19-year-old African American male. The suspect remains at-large and the investigation continues.  Released 02/22/2018 at 4:34 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
