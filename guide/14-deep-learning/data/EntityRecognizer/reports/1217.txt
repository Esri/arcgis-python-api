Members of the Dane County Narcotics Task Force recovered a loaded handgun while arresting a suspected heroin dealer on Camden Rd. Tuesday morning. The apprehension came following a several month investigation into local heroin peddling. The suspect was in a rental car when spotted by the Task Force. Members attempted to pull him over but he sped off. Later, he was seen entering a residence on Camden Rd. After about ten minutes, he peacefully came out and gave himself up.
