On 4-11-19, at approximately 10:16 pm, Madison Police Officers were dispatched to the 7900 block of Tree Lane on the west side of Madison. Approximately six shots were heard to the area southeast of 7900 Tree Lane. The area was checked and at this time, no evidence located, no injuries reported, and no damage to any property located. Released 04/11/2019 at 11:51 PM by Sgt. Ryan Gibson
