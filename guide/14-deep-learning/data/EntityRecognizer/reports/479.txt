Officers responded to an injury accident involving four vehicles. A vehicle traveling NB on Oak St caused a collision with two other vehicles resulting in a minor injury. When occupants from the original accident exited their vehicles several were injured when another vehicle struck one of the damaged vehicles sending it into them. One of the victims was transported to a local hospital with serious injuries. Two of drivers involved were arrested for OMVWI. This accident is still under investigation. Released 04/30/2016 at 10:02 AM by Lt. Timothy Strassman
