Officers are sent to the sounds of gunshots in the 4600 block of Verona Road.  Upon arrival they contact two men, one of whom tried to run from the police but was quickly apprehended.  The investigation reveals that the two men had come to the Verona Road location to sell prescription drugs to a third man.  This unidentified third man then tried to rob the first two men at gunpoint.  This unidentified man fired several gunshots at the first two men before fleeing the scene.  The one suspect who tried to run from the police, but was apprehended, was charged with several crimes.  The investigation is still on-going at this time.  Released 10/05/2016 at 4:29 AM by Sgt. Galen Wiering
