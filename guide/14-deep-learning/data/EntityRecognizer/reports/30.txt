 A 64-year-old woman was taken to a hospital with possible broken ribs after being struck by a vehicle while she was in a crosswalk at the intersection of University Ave. and Highland Ave. this morning. The driver, a 80-year-old man was cited for failure to yield. He had was turning east onto University from southbound Highland Ave. when he hit the pedestrian.
