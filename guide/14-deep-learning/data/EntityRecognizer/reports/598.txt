Green Cab responded to the area of 30 N Mills to pickup a fare with a requested destination on the North side of the city. When he arrives a suspect opens passenger door and pointed a gun at the driver demanding cash.  Driver produced cash but suspect demands more and strikes the driver in the head with the gun causing injury.  Driver flees and runs to Meriter Hospital to call as his phone was also stolen.  Suspect moved the cab, but it was recovered near location of occurrence.  Suspect still at large. Released 12/15/2018 at 9:21 PM by Sgt. Matthew Baker
