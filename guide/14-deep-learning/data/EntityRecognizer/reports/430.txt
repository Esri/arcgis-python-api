Madison Police responded on 5/5/2017 at 6:53p to Walgreens, 6601 McKee Rd. for a male creating a disturbance. Suspect, later identified as Nash, was reportedly screaming, harassing other customers, damaging store property, and punched an older male customer.Nash fled store prior to officer arrival. While driving west on McKee, Nash disregarded a traffic signal, and struck a Madison Police squad car at the intersection of McKee Rd/Maple Grove Dr. Officer transported to hospital for examination of injuries related to crash. Nash fled the crash scene and was captured by officers as he attempted to force entry to a nearby home.  Charges above. Released 05/06/2017 at 9:12 AM by Lt. Anthony Bitterman
