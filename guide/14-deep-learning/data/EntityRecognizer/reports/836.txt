The victim walked into their residence and two male suspects force their way into the victim's apartment.  The victim runs and locks themselves in a bathroom.  A short time later the victim begins smelling smoke.  Victim escapes through the bathroom window uninjured.  Madison Fire Department Investigators, and Madison Police Detectives are investigating the incident. The residence received fire and smoke damage. The Red Cross is attending to the displaced residents. If anyone has knowledge of the incident they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.  Released 05/24/2019 at 1:05 AM by Lt.
