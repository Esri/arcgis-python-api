Officers were dispatched to a business on the 7400 block of Mineral Point Rd for an armed robbery.  The suspect, described as wearing a black or dark hooded sweatshirt, dark pants and a dark scarf or cloth over his face pulled a handgun on the clerk, demanded money and fled an unknown direction.  There were no reported injuries, and the investigation is continuing. Released 09/29/2017 at 4:22 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
