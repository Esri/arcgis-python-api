  On 01/14/2018 at approximately 9:45 PM a van was stolen from an apartment complex parking lot in the 1800 block of Fordem Ave. Shortly after the van was taken, it was travelling outbound on East Washington Ave, when it struck another vehicle - injuring the occupants of this vehicle. The driver of the stolen automobile ran from the scene. The occupants of the victim vehicle were treated and their injuries are non-life threatening. If anyone knows the identity of the person that stole the van from the 1800 block of Fordem Ave and then caused an injury accident - please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers.   Released 01/15/2018 at 1:47 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
