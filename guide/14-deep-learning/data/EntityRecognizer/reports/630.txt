The Madison Police Department was dispatched to the 1200 block of MacArthur Rd after multiple callers reported hearing gunshots in the area. An adult male sustained a single non-life threatening gunshot wound and casings were recovered on scene. The suspect fled in an unknown vehicle. If anyone has any knowledge of the incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.  Released 01/13/2019 at 1:37 AM by Sgt. Nathaniel Lujan
