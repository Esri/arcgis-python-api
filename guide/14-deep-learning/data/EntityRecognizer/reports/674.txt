The Madison Police has responded to a report of an injured female in the near vicinity of N. Fair Oaks and Milwaukee Street.  A 29 year old female was found with a single gunshot wound to the chest.  She had been transported to a local hospital for treatment.  Her current status is unknown.  Information on the possible suspect(s) is currently being developed through the investigation by Madison Police personnel.  If anyone has any information regarding this incident, please contact the Madison Police Department.  Released 11/11/2018 at 10:25 PM by Lt. Daniel Nale
