 A smash-and-grab burglar got away with an estimated $12,000 worth of cigarettes following an early morning break-in at the BP gas station, 5551 Eastpark Blvd. The MPD responded to an alarm just before 4:00 a.m. Officers determined a cinder block had been used to shatter door glass. Some 150 cartons of cigarettes – with a retail value of about $80 each - are believed to have been stolen. Released 05/26/2016 at 10:41 AM by PIO Joel Despain
