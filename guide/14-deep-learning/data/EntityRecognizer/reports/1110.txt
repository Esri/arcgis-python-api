On 02/21/2018 at approximately 9:26 PM the Madison Police Department responded to the 3000 block of South Stoughton Road for a report of an armed robbery. The victim reported walking along the highway when he was confronted by an unknown white male, who appeared to be in his mid 30's. The male suspect was armed with a knife and an undisclosed amount of U.S. currency was taken. The victim was not physically injured and the suspect remains at-large.  The investigation continues. Released 02/22/2018 at 4:41 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
