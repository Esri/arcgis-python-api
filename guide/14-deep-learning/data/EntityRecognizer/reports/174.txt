 The MPD is seeking citizen help in locating a missing woman. She is Lynda Sharpe, age 67, Madison. Lynda is described as white, in her 60's, 5'9", 130 pounds, with brown hair and blue eyes. Lynda was last seen around 4:00 a.m. Wednesday in the area of Chamberlain Ave., which is near her home. She was  wearing a black and purple striped dress over gray sweat pants, and possibly a red coat. Anyone seeing Lynda should call 911. There is no reason to believe Lynda is the victim of a crime, but there is concern for her welfare.  Released 03/30/2016 at 3:11 PM by PIO Joel Despain
