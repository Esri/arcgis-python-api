At about 9:15pm a pizza delivery driver was robbed after he was walking back to his vehicle after making pizza deliveries at apartments on the 1100 Bl Morraine View Dr..  The suspect stuck a hard object believed to be a weapon in the back of the victim and demanded money.  After receiving an undisclosed amount the suspect fled on foot.  The victim was not injured. Released 04/22/2016 at 11:58 PM by Lt. John Radovan
