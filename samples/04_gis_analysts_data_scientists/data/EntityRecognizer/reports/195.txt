 Madison Police responded to a caller reporting someone was shot outside near Kipling Drive and Browning Road at 00:14.  This was a female caller and she sounded distraught however hung up on the communications center.   Officers checked the area and could not locate anyone injured in this area.  Officers further checked this general location on foot and no other callers or people reporting the incident were located.   At 00:41 hours a 21 year old male with a gunshot wound showed up at a local Hospital and indicated it occurred on Kipling Drive. The gunshot injury is believed to non life threatening at this time. Investigation continuing.   Released 11/10/2017 at 3:27 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
