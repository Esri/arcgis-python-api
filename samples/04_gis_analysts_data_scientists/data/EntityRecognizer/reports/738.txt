 A bouncer was punched several times in the face and placed in a headlock Friday night after denying an intoxicated man entry into the Nitty Gritty, 223 N. Frances St. A second employee helped the victim gain control of the man as police were called. The bouncer was not seriously injured and the suspect was arrested for battery.
