 Madison Police were called to the 3300 block of Milwaukee Street at 23:16 for a male breaking into a vehicle. Responding officers were able to contact a subject near Milwaukee Street and St. Paul who matched the provided description and was carrying a garbage can.  The theft from vehicle involved over $500 worth of candy taken from a vehicle (vehicle owner is candy distributor) and the 'sweets' were in the garbage can the suspect had with him. A 55 year old male was taken into custody and transported to Dane County Jail.   Released 12/19/2017 at 4:31 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
