The MPD is investigating a daytime shooting in the 2300 block of Huxley St. Several callers reported hearing multiple gunshots around 9:20 a.m. Thus far, officers have recovered three shell casings from the roadway. There have been no reports of injuries or property damage. Some of the witnesses saw a dark colored SUV speeding from the area just as reports were heard.  Released 12/14/2017 at 10:07 AM by PIO Joel Despain
