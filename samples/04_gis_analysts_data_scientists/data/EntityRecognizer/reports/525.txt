 Officers were called to the 4700 block of Cottage Grove Rd. this morning for reports of a driver pointing a handgun out of the window of a car. An officer spotted the car speeding from Cottage Grove Rd. onto Acewood Blvd. There, the driver abandoned the car and fled on foot. A perimeter was established, and Stoughton Police brought in a K9; however, no suspect could be located. Released 08/22/2018 at 2:29 PM by PIO Joel Despain
