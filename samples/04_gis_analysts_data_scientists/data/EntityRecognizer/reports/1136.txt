Officers were sent to the 1700 block of Northport Drive reference a stabbing.  Officers arrived and discovered 3 injured parties, with information that another had fled the area.  Two of the injured were treated on scene while the third was transported to an area hospital for a non-life-threatening laceration.  Officers searched the area and with the help of citizen callers to 911, located the male victim with multiple significant lacerations and significant bleeding.  He was transported to an area hospital.  Further investigation revealed the suspect, Edmond, had an altercation with the male victim which resulted in these injuries.  The investigation is continuing, and there presently appears to be no further danger to the public.  Released 09/01/2017 at 5:07 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
