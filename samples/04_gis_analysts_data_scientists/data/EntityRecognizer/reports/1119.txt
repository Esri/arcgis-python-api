Officers responded to multiple callers reporting shots fired near the 400 block of East Wilson.  Investigation reveals that a disturbance occurred inside an establishment in this area when involved parties moved outside.  Shots were fired and people fled.  Officers located multiple shell casings and approximately 7 vehicles damaged at this location.  One of these damaged vehicles was occupied at the time, but it is not believed that individual or vehicle were targeted.  At the time of this release, there are no known injuries.  Investigation continuing. Released 09/02/2017 at 4:48 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
