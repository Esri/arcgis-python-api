On 02/21/2018 at approximately 6:27 PM the Madison Police Department responded to 6702 Raymond Road (Kwik Trip) for a report of a shooting. This investigation continues, but it appears that this was a targeted shooting and the suspects remain at-large.  At this time, it appears that no persons were struck by gunfire.  The Madison Police Department is looking into the possibility that this shooting may be connected to the shooting that occurred on 02/21/2018 at 7;49 PM in the 4200 block of the West Beltline Hwy frontage road. Released 02/22/2018 at 4:18 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
