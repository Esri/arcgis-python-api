The victim was walking home in the 500 block W Wilson St when she was approached from behind by an unknown suspect.  The suspect groped the victim over the outside of her clothing, forcefully running his hands over the victims upper legs and breast area.  Suspect fled when victim began to scream and was last seen fleeing northbound on foot between the residences in the 500 block W Wilson St.Suspect could only be described as a male, wearing a red or dark colored hooded sweatshirt, dark jacket and pants, and gloves.Victim was not physically injured as a result of this incident.
