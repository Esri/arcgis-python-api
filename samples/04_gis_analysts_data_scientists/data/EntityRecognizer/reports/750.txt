 Madison Police responded to Woodman's West for a robbery where two victims were pepper sprayed and a purse was taken.  A male and female were accosted in the parking lot while leaving the store, struck multiple times in the face and pepper sprayed before the purse was taken during this robbery.  The suspects were decscribed 4-5 black females who were last seen leaving in a SUV.  This vehicle was stolen earlier today from the west side.  The victims were transported to an area hospital for the pepper spray exposure and facial injuries.    Released 12/16/2016 at 2:14 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
