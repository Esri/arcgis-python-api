 A 36-year-old Madison man has been arrested in connection with a fight where he allegedly sliced another man in the face with a knife. The suspect and victim are family members and got into a dispute early yesterday morning inside a home on N. Meadow Lane. The victim needed more than 20 stitches to close cuts. He did not immediately report the incident, but did tell police about it yesterday afternoon. Released 05/03/2016 at 1:56 PM by PIO Joel Despain
