Madison Police responded to Demetral Park reference an armed robbery.  The suspect was holding a knife and demanded that the victim give him everything the victim had.  The suspect also implied he had a gun.  When the victim told the suspect he did not have anything to give, the suspect slashed at the victim, causing a minor injury.  The suspect took a clothing item from the victim. This investigation is ongoing.  Anyone with information regarding this incident is encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crimestoppers.  Released 06/27/2018 at 1:12 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
