Two juveniles were arrested for stealing a car from the city of Fitchburg. Madison dispatchers had announced that a vehicle was stolen from the city of Fitchburg. Not long after the vehicle was  stolen, Madison Officers saw the vehicle on Mineral Point Rd near Randolph Dr, and initiated a stop with that vehicle. The vehicle slid into a snow bank and the driver ran away. The passenger was arrested on scene, and officers followed the foot prints in the snow and the driver was located and arrested close by. The driver was charged with resisting arrest and theft of an automobile. The passenger was also arrested for charges related to the stolen automobile. Both were taken to the Juvenile Reception Center. Released 01/28/2019 at 2:41 AM by Sgt. Ryan Gibson
