 An Iowa man was arrested for battery after he jumped on the back of a downtown bar manager and began punching the victim in the head. He also pushed a finger into the victim's eye. The Church Key employee was in the middle of breaking up a disturbance when he was assaulted. One of those involved in the altercation had come to the tavern with the suspect.
