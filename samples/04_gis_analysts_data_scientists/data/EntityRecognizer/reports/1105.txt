MPD officers responded to a female at the West Towne Mall who reported receiving disturbing and threatening electronic messages from an ex-partner. At least one of the messages included a photo of a handgun. Officers worked with neighboring jurisdictions and located the sender of the messages, Justin W Riley, driving his vehicle near Verona Rd and McKee/PD. Officers conducted a traffic stop of Riley and found a handgun in the vehicle. Riley was arrested on charges of Disorderly Conduct, Unauthorized Use of Computerized Device, and Operating a Motor Vehicle while Intoxicated-3rd Offense. Released 03/25/2018 at 5:03 PM by Lt. Mindy Winter
