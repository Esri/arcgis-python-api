On 01/01/2018 at approximately 4:30 PM the Bassett Food Mart was robbed at gunpoint by an unknown white male suspect. The suspect is described as being tall with a thin build, dressed in dark clothing. The suspect remains at-large and the Madison Police Department is looking into the fact that this may be the second robbery committed on this date by the same suspect. Released 01/02/2018 at 2:53 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
