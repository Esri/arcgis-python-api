 A woman, who had her car stolen from her Sinykin Circle driveway Friday night, called the MPD when she spotted her Ford Taurus parked this morning outside Walmart, 4198 Nakoosa Trail. The MPD located the man who had been driving it inside the store. He had a key to the car in his pocket and was arrested for operating a motor vehicle without owner's consent.
