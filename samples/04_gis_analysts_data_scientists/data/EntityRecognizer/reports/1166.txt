A Madison Metro bus left the roadway this afternoon and ran into a brick wall that houses trash cans just outside of the MPD's North District, 2033 Londonderry Dr. MPD detectives were first on the scene and provided emergency aid to the driver and two passengers. It's believed the driver experienced some sort of medical emergency as the bus was traveling on nearby Packers Ave. He was taken to a hospital. One passenger was also transported with minor injuries. There were a total of five passengers on the bus at the time of the accident, which is under investigation    Released 10/23/2018 at 2:25 PM by PIO Joel Despain
