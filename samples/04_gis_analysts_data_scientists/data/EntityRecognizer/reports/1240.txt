 Concerned neighbors helped pull an intoxicated woman out of the Yahara River after the 32-year-old Oregon, WI woman jumped in following a traffic crash. Witnesses said the SUV the woman was driving left the roadway on Riverside Dr., went up a bike path entrance, over some rocks, before hitting a tree near the river. She was not seriously hurt but was taken to a hospital. She will be cited for first offense drunken driving. Released 09/24/2018 at 2:05 PM by PIO Joel Despain
