East High's School Resource Officer (SRO) was notified Thursday about a sexual assault that took place inside the school Wednesday afternoon. A 15-year-old student told the SRO she was sexually assaulted by two male students inside a bathroom sometime around 4:00 p.m. One suspect was arrested last night on a tentative charge of second degree sexual assault of a child. The second suspect was taken into custody by East's SRO this morning. He is also facing a tentative charge of second degree sexual assault of a child.  Released 04/12/2019 at 12:47 PM by PIO Joel Despain
