Madison Police officers responded to a hold up alarm activation at the Home Savings Bank. Officers quickly learned that the bank had just been robbed by a male who was carrying what was described as a purse and that the male left the bank on foot. A Parking Enforcement Officer in the area spotted a male matching this general description running from the area of the bank. MPD officers caught up with and detained this individual and he is considered a person of interest in the robbery. Investigation is on-going. Released 04/27/2018 at 4:52 PM by Lt. Mindy Winter
