A 20-year-old was walking home after withdrawing money at a nearby ATM. He was approached by an adult male in a hooded sweatshirt that demanded his money. The suspect and victim struggled, and the victim was hit in the back of the head with an object causing him to go unconscious. The victim suffered minor injuries. Released 05/02/2017 at 2:22 AM by Sgt. Eugene Woehrle
