The Madison Police Department was dispatched to Kelley's Mobile gas station located at 2601 Shopko Drive.  A male suspect entered the Kelley's Mobile with a handgun and robbed the gas station of money.  The suspect fled in an unknown direction. If anyone has any knowledge of the incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program. Released 01/08/2019 at 3:18 AM by Lt. Reginald Patterson
