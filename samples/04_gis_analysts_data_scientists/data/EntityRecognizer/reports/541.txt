Madison Police were called to the area of the 6700 block of Schroeder Road for a man seen walking toward an apartment building with a gun.  While officers were checking the area another call came in regarding a person who was shot during a robbery in this same area. Police located a 26 year old male with a gunshot wound inside an apartment building and he was transported to a local hospital for his injuries.  There are believed to multiple suspects in this weapons incident who fled from the scene.  If anyone has information regarding this shooting incident please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers with information.  updated information:The suspects in this incident are described as two black males, 18 - 25 years old and fled on foot prior to officers finding the injured victim.   Released 01/08/2016 at 1:44 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
