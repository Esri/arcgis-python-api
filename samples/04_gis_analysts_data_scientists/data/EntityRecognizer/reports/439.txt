Madison Police officers were dispatched to the 600 block of Constitution Ln in the Midtown district of Madison,for a residential burglary investigation. Officers arrive on scene and find evidence of entry into a residence in the 600 block. A Madison K9 officer was utilized but no suspect was located. A male wearing dark clothing was seen in the area about one hour before the burglary call, wearing dark clothing and a backpack. The residence was checked and no suspects located inside. Released 03/24/2019 at 6:06 AM by Sgt. Ryan Gibson
