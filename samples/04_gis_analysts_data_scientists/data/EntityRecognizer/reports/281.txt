Madison Police were called for an armed robbery that occurred near Glenwood Park on Madisons west side.  Two subjects were robbed at gunpoint of backpacks, cell phones and wallets.  One suspect displayed a silver handgun during this incident but no injuries were reported. If you have any information about this incident or suspects please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers.   Released 06/18/2016 at 3:52 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
