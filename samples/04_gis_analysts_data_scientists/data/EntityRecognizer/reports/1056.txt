 A 23-year-old Madison man suffered head injuries after falling 13-feet from a downtown parking ramp early Sunday morning. Witnesses said the victim punched at the window of a departing car prior to going over a railing at the State Street Campus Parking Garage. He landed on Hawthorne Court. Alcohol is believed to be a contributing factor in case. Released 01/22/2018 at 10:23 AM by PIO Joel Despain
