 An 87-year-old man was out for an early morning walk Saturday when he saw someone fire several shots out of a dark-colored SUV with tinted windows. The witness was near the intersection of Cottage Grove Rd. and Dempsey Rd. when gunfire broke out. There were no reports of injuries or property damage. Responding officers recovered four shell casings. Released 06/17/2019 at 9:38 AM by PIO Joel Despain
