Victims were in their apartment when suspect #2 knocks on the door.  When the male victim answers it suspect #2 motions for suspect #1 to come to the door, which he does armed with a handgun.  They force their way into the apartment where they rob the victims at gunpoint of personal items.  The suspects fled on foot and a K9 track was unsuccesful.  The victims 5 yr old and 1 yr old children were present and the 5 yr old witnessed the incident.  None of the victims were physically harmed. Released 07/04/2016 at 3:36 AM by Lt. John Radovan
