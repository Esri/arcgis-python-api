The Madison Police Department was called to the Milio's restaurant at 462 Commerce Drive reference an armed robbery.  The victim, the lone employee at the store, was not injured.  The suspect (see above for description) was armed with a black handgun, and he ordered the victim to hand over the currency in the register.  The male fled in an unknown direction with an undisclosed amount of US Currency.  If anyone has any information reference this incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.  Released 11/01/2018 at 11:36 PM by Lt. Daniel Nale
