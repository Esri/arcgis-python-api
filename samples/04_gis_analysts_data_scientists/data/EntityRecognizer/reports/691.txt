Two masked men, at least one of whom was armed with a gun, pulled off an early morning robbery at PLS Check Cashing, 1907 South Park St. The intruders took off with the store's money and appear to have fled to a getaway car that was parked nearby. Anyone with information on this crime can contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at (608) 266-6014. Released 02/20/2019 at 8:38 AM by PIO Joel Despain
