 The MPD arrested a 41-year-old Madison man for his fifth offense of operating while under the influence following a minor two-car crash on Commercial Ave. Sunday afternoon. Witnesses said the victim, a 23-year-old Madison man, was turning onto N. Sprecher Rd. when his car was struck. He had been driving westbound on Commercial. The suspect was heading eastbound.   Released 04/11/2016 at 3:28 PM by PIO Joel Despain
