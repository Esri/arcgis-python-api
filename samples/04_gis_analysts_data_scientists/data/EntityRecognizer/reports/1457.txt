The Madison Police Department is investigating an incident in which a male was walking in the 2600 Block of E. Washington Ave when he was robbed of an undisclosed amount of cash.  One suspect displayed a knife and the other was wielding what the victim believed to be a pipe.  The victim was struck with that object. This investigation is ongoing.  Anyone with information regarding this incident is encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crimestoppers.  Released 07/25/2018 at 4:21 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
