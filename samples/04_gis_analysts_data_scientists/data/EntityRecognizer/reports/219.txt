 The Burglary Crimes Unit is seeking your help in locating a burglary suspect who has a long history of similar criminal behavior. Carlo D. Walkes, age 32, is wanted for breaking out the window of a car early Wednesday. It was parked in an underground lot on N. Park St. There is also an active probation warrant out for his arrest. Walkes is a person of interest in several recent burglaries and thefts that have taken place in the W. Washington Ave. and Park St. corridors. Anyone with information on his whereabouts is asked to contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at (608) 266-6014. Anyone seeing Walkes should call 911.  Released 04/25/2019 at 2:28 PM by PIO Joel Despain
