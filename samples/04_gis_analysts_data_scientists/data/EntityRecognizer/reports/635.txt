 The MPD is asking for citizen help in identifying the man seen in the attached surveillance photo. On Monday afternoon, he stole a plastic donation jar, filled with money. The funds were to go to Second Harvest Foodbank, as the not for profit assists families this holiday season. Anyone with information on who the thief might be should contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014. He could be associated with a gray Chevrolet Cavalier.
