A victim riding his bicycle on the Capital City Bike Trail was confronted by an unidentified male of unknown race, who was also riding a bike when initially observed.  The suspect displayed a black handgun and demanded the victim give up his wallet and then throw his cellular phone away from him.  The suspect took the wallet from the scene. This incident is believed to be related to incident number 2018-237817, another armed robbery.  This incident remains under investigation.  Anyone with information concerning this incident is encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crimestoppers. Released 06/26/2018 at 1:31 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
