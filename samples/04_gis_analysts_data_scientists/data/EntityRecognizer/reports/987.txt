 A MPD sergeant, on routine patrol, was one of several who heard four to five gunshots early this morning near Milwaukee St. Callers from Union St. and Hermina St. were among those calling 911. One Hermina St. resident said he could hear men arguing in the roadway. He saw two SUV's drive away quickly, and seconds later heard shots. There were no immediate reports of property damage or injuries. Released 06/02/2016 at 9:47 AM by PIO Joel Despain
