MPD officers were dispatched to a traffic crash involving a vehicle and a pedestrian in the eastbound lanes of Northport Dr. at Barby Ln.  The driver (F, 37 yoa, Madison) struck the pedestrian (F, 13 yoa, Madison) as she walked across Northport Dr.  The pedestrian was transported to a local hospital with non-life-threatening injuries.  Investigation continuing.
