Officers were dispatched to the report of multiple shots fired with people fleeing the area. Upon arrival officers locate a male in the parking lot with gunshot wounds. He was transported to a local hospital, treated, and released. Another male who apparently had sustained gunshot wounds showed up at a local hospital. He is being treated for serious injuries. The investigation is in the early stages, but there is no information at this point that indicates it is related to any previous weapons offense. If anyone has information on this incident please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014. Released 05/22/2016 at 8:00 AM by Lt. Timothy Strassman
