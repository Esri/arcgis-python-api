 A 40-year-old Madison man told police he was robbed of his wallet early Sunday morning in the 500 block of N. Pinckney St. The victim said he was in the passenger seat of a car when a stranger opened the door and forcibly removed his wallet from a pocket. The victim quickly cancelled his credit cards, and told officers that there had been no reports of any fraudulent transactions. Released 01/14/2019 at 3:15 PM by PIO Joel Despain
