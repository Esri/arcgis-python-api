Officers responded to the 900 block of Blaine Dr. for a home invasion.  Detectives reported that this was a targeted incident.  3 suspects entered the victim's home and one of them pointed a facsimile firearm at the victim.  The suspects took the victim's cell phone and money.  The suspects fled and 2 of the suspects were apprehended behind Mendota Elementary.  The school was briefly placed on lockdown.  The third suspect is still at large.  The investigation is ongoing. Released 05/12/2017 at 7:17 PM by Sgt. Jamar Gary
