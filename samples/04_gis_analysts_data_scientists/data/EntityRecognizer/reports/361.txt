Witness statements indicate the male pedestrian had been walk irradically and proceeded to walking into the roadway on Williamson St.  It is believed the pedestrian lost his balance and fell possibly hitting his head.  As he lay on the roadway the pedestrian was struck by a vehicle traveling on Williamson St.  Victim transported to the hospital and it is believed, at this time, the injuries are not considered life threatening.Traffic was temporarily shut down in a one block section of Williamson St but has since been reopened.The driver of the vehicle that struck the pedestrian is cooperating with the investigation.
