The Madison Police Dept. is currently on scene and is investigating a single vehicle vs. tree crash near the intersection of Prairie Rd. and Birch Hill Dr.  There is one confirmed fatality.  It's believed that there were no other occupants in the vehicle at the time of the crash.  Traffic is being diverted out of the area as the investigation continues. Released 07/14/2017 at 2:47 AM by Sgt. Kurt Wege
