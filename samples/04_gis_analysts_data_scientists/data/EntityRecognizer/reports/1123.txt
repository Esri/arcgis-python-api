On 6/10/18 at 1:50 am the Madison Police Department was dispatched to the 2000 block of Post Rd.  A 58 year old man was found deceased in a residence.  It will be up to the Dane County Medical Examiner (ME) to determine the cause and manner of the death.  The investigation is ongoing.
