The MPD's Burglary Crimes Unit will be investigating a series of smash-and-grab burglaries from early this morning. The MPD has been notified of the following business break-ins: Simply Communications, 1903 S. Park St. Northside Liquor, 2801 N. Sherman Ave. Cricket Wireless, 4613 Verona Rd. T-Mobile, 2608 E. Washington Ave. Misty Mountain Games, 4672 Cottage Grove Rd. In some cases, phones and cash were taken. Investigators believe several cases are connected, and it's possible the same criminal(s) could be responsible for the entire burglary spree.Anyone with information on these crimes is urged to contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at (608) 266-6014.   Released 02/27/2019 at 9:38 AM by PIO Joel Despain
