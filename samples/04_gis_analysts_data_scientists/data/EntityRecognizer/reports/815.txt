Two individuals were seated in a vehicle in the area of Great Gray Drive and Owl Creek Drive when an individual reportedly approached the car and fired multiple rounds at the vehicle, striking it with more than one round.  Victim vehicle left the scene and police were called, making contact with both occupants in a separate location.  Officers responding to the scene were able to locate at least one shell casing in the described location.  No other property damage was located, and there were no reported injuries. This investigation is continuing. Released 04/27/2017 at 1:34 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
