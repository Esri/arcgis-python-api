MPD responded to the intersection of Vilas Ave. and Harrison St. reference a vehicle that crashed into an unoccupied parked vehicle.  Officers contacted the driver F, W, 71 yoa, Madison.  The driver was traveling on Vilas Ave. when she veered over the center line.  The driver overcorrected and swerved into the parked vehicle.  The impact caused the driver's vehicle to flip onto the roof.  The driver was transported to a local hospital for minor injuries.  Alcohol and/or drugs did not appear to be a factor.  The driver was not cited. Released 08/30/2018 at 5:54 PM by Lt. Jamar Gary
