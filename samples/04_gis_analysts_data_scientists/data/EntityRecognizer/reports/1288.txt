   A DeForest man was not seriously hurt when his car was t-boned by another as a drunken driver ran a red light on John Nolen Dr. Saturday night. The collision, which took place at the intersection with North Shore Dr., left a debris field that temporarily blocked part of the roadway. The arrested driver admitted he had been drinking at a downtown bar prior to heading out John Nolen Dr. He was issued citations which are listed above.
