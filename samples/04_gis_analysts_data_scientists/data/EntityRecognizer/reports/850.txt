 Officers responded to a burglary alarm early this morning and found there had been a smash-and-grab break-in at Tech Heroes, 3769 E. Washington Ave. Not only was front door glass shattered, but so was glass that had enclosed two display cases. An estimated 25 cell phones worth around $5,000 were stolen. Surveillance video shows three men entered the store. Released 10/17/2017 at 9:24 AM by PIO Joel Despain
