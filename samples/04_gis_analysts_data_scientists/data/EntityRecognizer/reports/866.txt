The Madison Police Department responded to the report of an armed robbery that occurred at the Mobil Mart on N. Sherman Avenue today.  The clerk, who was not injured, told police that a masked African American male with the description listed above came in to the store brandishing a handgun and demanding money.  The suspect was given an undisclosed amount of money and some additional property, and he then left.  The Madison Police Department is asking anyone with information on this incident to contact the Department, or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.  Released 10/07/2018 at 10:34 PM by Lt. Daniel Nale
