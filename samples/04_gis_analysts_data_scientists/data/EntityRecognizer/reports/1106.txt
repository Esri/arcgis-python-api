Shortly before 7:00 PM, Madison Police, along with Fitchburg PD and Town of Madison PD responded to the area of 2900 Todd Drive for multiple reports of shots fired.  Several vehicles were seen driving away from the area at a high rate of speed.  While Officers were investigating this incident, more shots were heard near Post Road.  At this point there are no reported injuries and Officers are currently canvassing the area looking for any potential property damage. Released 04/25/2016 at 8:41 PM by Sgt. Nicholas Ellis
