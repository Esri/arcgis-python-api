 Someone with a BB gun damaged numerous windows at Stalzy's Deli, 2701 Atwood Ave. An employee discovered the damage yesterday morning. A delivery vehicle's windshield had also been hit. An investigating officer estimated 60 to 70 rounds of BBs had been shot at the business and the vehicle. The MPD is seeking citizen help in identifying the person, or persons responsible for the criminal damage. Callers can contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at (608) 266-6014.
