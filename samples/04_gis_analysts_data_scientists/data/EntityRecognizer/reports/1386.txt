 The MPD detained a bank robbery suspect during a traffic stop after U.S. Bank, 4602 East Towne Blvd., was held up just before 1:00 p.m. Witnesses told police the robber - who did not display a weapon - left in a cab. The taxi was pulled over near E. Washington Ave. and Schmedeman Ave. and the person of interest was detained without incident. Detectives are currently speaking with the suspect. Released 12/13/2016 at 3:02 PM by PIO Joel Despain
