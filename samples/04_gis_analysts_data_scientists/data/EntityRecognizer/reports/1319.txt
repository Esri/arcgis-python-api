 Concerned residents, living near Windigo Trail and Pontiac Trail, heard four to five gunshots, followed by the sounds of squealing car tires early this morning. Responding officers found shell casings and canvassed the area. They were unable to find any property damage, although it was still dark. There were no reports of injuries.    Released 08/07/2018 at 10:49 AM by PIO Joel Despain
