MPD officers responded to the 2900 block of Todd Dr. reference a white Dodge Journey that was stolen at gunpoint.  The occupants of the vehicle reported that the robber walked up to the van and knocked on the window.  The robber was armed with a handgun and ordered the occupants out of the vehicle.  The suspect then fled in the vehicle.  With the assistance of the registered owner, officers were able to track the van via GPS to the 7500 block of Tree Ln.  Upon arrival, officers found the vehicle unoccupied.  No injuries were reported in this incident. Released 11/20/2018 at 8:46 PM by Lt. Jamar Gary
