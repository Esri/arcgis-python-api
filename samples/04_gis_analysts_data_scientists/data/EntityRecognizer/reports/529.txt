  A Madison man suffered a broken nose after being punched by a stranger inside a downtown bar early Saturday morning. The victim said his attacker first bumped into him at Red Rock Saloon, 322 W. Johnson St., and then - unprovoked - delivered the blow. Madison Fire Department paramedics responded to provide first aid to the victim. A suspect has not yet been identified.  Released 04/04/2016 at 10:53 AM by PIO Joel Despain
