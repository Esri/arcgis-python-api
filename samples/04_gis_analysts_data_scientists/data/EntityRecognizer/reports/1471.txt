 A burglar used a brick to shatter a window at the Packer Liquor Store, 3506 Packers Ave. Once inside the criminal grabbed alcohol and cigarettes, stashing the loot inside a duffle bag. Surveillance video indicates the intruder was inside less than two minutes and that the intrusion occurred around 11:20 p.m. last night. The break-in was called in around 7:23 this morning after the crime was discovered. Released 12/09/2016 at 11:11 AM by PIO Joel Despain
