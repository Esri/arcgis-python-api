 Madison Police responded at 22:10 to the 500 block of South Park Street for a attempted armed robbery. A 35 year old female employee of EDO Asian Cuisine was outside near the back lot when she was approached by a male with a hatchet who attempted to rob her.  This female only spoke Mandarian Chinese and the suspect ended up fleeing on foot without obtaining anything.  No injuries were reported. The suspect was an unknown race male, 6'02", skinny,  wearing all black clothing and something over his face.  Investigation continuing.  Released 12/26/2017 at 5:39 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
