 Officers were sent to a State Street restaurant early this morning for a report of a man picking fights with diners. When officers arrived, they witnessed Philemon R. Dillard, age 46, Madison, throwing punches at a patron inside Pizza Di Roma, 313 State St. Dillard was arrested for battery and disorderly conduct. Earlier in the night, Dillard had been the subject of three other police calls as he was yelling profanities at passersby on State Street. Released 05/17/2018 at 8:28 AM by PIO Joel Despain
