The Dane County Narcotics Task Force, with assistance from MPD SWAT, arrested a 50-year-old N. Thompson Dr. resident yesterday for maintaining a place for drug trafficking. During a search of his home heroin, marijuana, digital scales, pills, and drug paraphernalia were seized. The suspect is accused of selling both prescription medications and heroin out of his apartment which detectives found had multiple hidden video cameras. Released 08/16/2018 at 3:38 PM by PIO Joel Despain
