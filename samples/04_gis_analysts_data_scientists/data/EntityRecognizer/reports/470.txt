A 41-year-old Madison man has been taken into custody on a tentative charge of possession of child pornography. Jeffrey S. Hembrook was arrested as the MPD's SWAT team executed a search warrant in the 4700 Block of Catalina Parkway.  His apprehension is part of an ongoing investigation. It is being conducted by MPD Special Victims Unit (SVU) detectives with assistance from Wisconsin Department of Justice Internet Crimes Against Children (ICAC) Task Force special agents. This is an open investigation and more tentative charges are likely.      Released 08/24/2017 at 3:10 PM by PIO Joel Despain
