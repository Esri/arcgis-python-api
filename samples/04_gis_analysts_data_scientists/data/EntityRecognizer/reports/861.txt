Employees of La Bamba's restaurant had closed for the night and were finishing their closing duties.  One employee briefly unlocked a door for the business when he was immediately confronted by a suspect pointing a handgun at him.  Employee was ordered back into the store and both employees were forced to lay on the floor.  Suspect stole cash.  One employee had minor injuries from being pushed to the ground by the suspect.Suspect left the store on foot in an unknown direction. Released 10/16/2016 at 5:28 AM by Lt. Anthony Fiore
