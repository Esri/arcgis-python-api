On 11/23/2017, at approximately 11:22am, several callers reported multiple shots fired in the 1700 block of Onsgard Rd. Officers learned that there were several people involved and at least two people were armed with handguns. Officers located shell casings in the area. No one was reported to be injured, but there was property damage. The investigation continues. Released 11/23/2017 at 8:47 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
