At approximately 11:10 p.m. the Madison Police Department responded to the intersection of Schroeder Rd /Struck St. for a male causing a disturbance. When officers arrive they witness the suspect run toward the 6500 block of Schroeder Rd. Officers learn that the suspect forced entry into an occupied dwelling and assault the resident. The suspect remains inside the residence and maybe in possession of a weapon. MPD SWAT is now on scene and has established a perimeter at the residence. The investigation is ongoing. If anyone has knowledge of the incident they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.   Released 02/27/2019 at 2:39 AM by Lt. Reginald Patterson
