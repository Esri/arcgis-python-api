 Officers responded to a two-car car crash on Williamson St. Saturday night. The driver of one car - who refused to identify himself - would later be arrested for his fourth offense of drunken driving. A passenger in his car, and the driver of the other, were both taken to the hospital with minor injuries. Officers were able to positively identify the arrested man during the booking process at the Dane County Jail. Released 05/21/2018 at 9:53 AM by PIO Joel Despain
