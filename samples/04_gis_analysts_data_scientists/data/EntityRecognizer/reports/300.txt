 A 29-year-old Madison man suffered a non life-threatening stab wound to the torso after a social gathering inside a Sherman Terrace residence deteriorates into violence Saturday night. It started with an argument. The victim says the suspect next armed himself with a kitchen knife and a beer bottle. He says he was struck with the bottle and stabbed with the knife. The injured man went to a hospital while the suspect ended up in jail. Released 01/30/2017 at 12:31 PM by PIO Joel Despain
