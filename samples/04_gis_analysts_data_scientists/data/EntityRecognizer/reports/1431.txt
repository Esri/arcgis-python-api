 A restaurant employee was found unconscious last night after he was knocked out during an armed robbery behind Swagat Indian Restaurant, 707 N. High Point Rd. The victim had gone out to a restaurant vehicle when he was attacked from behind by two men. He said one was armed with a handgun and he was punched multiple times. His empty wallet was found near where he lay. The victim did not regain consciousness until he was at a hospital. Cash and a debit card were stolen during the armed robbery. Released 10/31/2016 at 11:03 AM by PIO Joel Despain
