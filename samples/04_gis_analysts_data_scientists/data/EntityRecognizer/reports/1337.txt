 A 22-year-old man was taken to a hospital after being pistol whipped, punched, and stomped on in the 6800 block of Park Ridge Dr. Tuesday afternoon. The victim had driven into the area with another man and was outside of the car when he was attacked by several men. His phone was taken during the incident. The victim provided limited information to the MPD. Detectives believe this was a targeted attack and robbery. Released 12/05/2018 at 9:31 AM by PIO Joel Despain
