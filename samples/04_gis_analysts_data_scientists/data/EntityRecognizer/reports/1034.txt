 Multiple officers responded to a large disturbance, outside in the 1900 block of Cameron Dr., Monday afternoon. Several people were reported fighting. Some were punching; others were slapping. One person damaged a parked car, another deployed pepper spray, and a pregnant woman said she was struck in the stomach.  One involved party said the melee broke out after threats and disrespectful comments were made on social media.  Released 06/27/2017 at 11:48 AM by PIO Joel Despain
