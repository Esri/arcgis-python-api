The Madison Police Department was dispatched to the area around Connections Bar reference numerous calls of a shooting.  Upon arrival, officers did discover evidence of a shooting that occurred in the parking lot of the establishment.  At this time, there have been no injuries reported as a result of this incident.  Based on the investigation so far, it is unclear what precipitated the shots being fired.  This investigation is ongoing.  If anyone has any information regarding this incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program. Released 10/07/2018 at 3:08 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
