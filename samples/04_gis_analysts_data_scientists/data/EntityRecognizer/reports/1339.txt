At 6:42 PM Madison Police received a complaint of a subject being battered in the 1400 block of Troy Drive by 3 male blacks and 1 female black.  All parties left the scene prior to police arrival.  The Madison Police Department is attempting to identify the victim in this incident and a female black subject, who was wearing a white t-shirt and blue shorts, that accompanied the victim.  Anyone with information pertaining to this incident should contact the Madison Police Department or contact Crimestoppers at 266-6014.
