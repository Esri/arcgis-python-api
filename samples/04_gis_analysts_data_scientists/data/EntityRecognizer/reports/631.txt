 A gunman robbed U.S. Bank, 4602 East Towne Blvd., this afternoon. His description is similar to those provided by victims at several other recent bank robberies. In this case, a black handgun was displayed as the man demanded money. He fled with some of the bank's cash. Officers are currently in the area searching for clues and a suspect. Released 02/22/2018 at 2:44 PM by PIO Joel Despain
