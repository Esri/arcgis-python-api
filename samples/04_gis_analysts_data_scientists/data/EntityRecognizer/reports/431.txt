Officers were dispatched to an armed robbery at the PDQ (7502 Mineral Point Road). The suspect entered the store, demanded money from the clerk and implied that he had a gun. The suspect fled from the store on foot. Officers responded, set a perimeter, and a K9 track was attempted. Suspect was not located. No injuries reported. Investigation on-going. Released 06/27/2017 at 2:16 AM by Sgt. Eugene Woehrle
