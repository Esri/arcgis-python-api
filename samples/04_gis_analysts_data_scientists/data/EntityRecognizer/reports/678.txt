 Madison Police were called at 8:56 pm for an armed robbery that occurred at the AutoZone in the 3500 block of East Washington Avenue.  The suspect entered the store and ordered two employees to the back room at gunpoint.  The suspect then ordered one employee to open the safe before fleeing on foot with an undisclosed amount of money. No one was injured during this armed robbery and the suspect fled on foot from the area.  Investigation is ongoing at this time. If you have any information about this robbery please contact the Madison Police Department or call Madison Area Crime Stoppers.   Released 11/07/2018 at 3:02 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
