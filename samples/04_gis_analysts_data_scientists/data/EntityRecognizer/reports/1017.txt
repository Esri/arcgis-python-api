 Responding officers recovered a shell casing following reports of gunfire Sunday night near the Citgo gas station, 1423 Northport Dr. Callers believed there had been a dispute between several men, men who had gotten into a couple of different cars. At least one shot was fired as combatants drove from the area. There were no reports of injuries or property damage. Released 09/26/2016 at 9:43 AM by PIO Joel Despain
