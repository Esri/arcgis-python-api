 A 54-year-old woman suffered significant facial injuries and had bruising around her neck after being attacked and robbed inside a Perry St. motel room Sunday night. The victim said a man and a woman battered and choked her, and that she lost consciousness several times. The male suspect is known to her, but not the female. All had been socializing in the room prior to the violence. The suspects have not been located. Released 11/19/2018 at 12:14 PM by PIO Joel Despain
