?Several Witnesses called in reporting gunshots in a parking lot for an apartment building.   Witnesses reported seeing one subject possibly injured get into a silver vehicle which left. We have not received any reports of injuries or victims as a result of this incident.  A perimeter was established and a K9 track was conducted ending a short distance away.  As Officers were clearing the scene, a vehicle arrived at a nearby address with a bullet hole in the side of the car.  A loaded 40 caliber handguns with extended magazine was found in the vehicle, however it is unknow if the vehicle, occupants or recovered handgun are related to this investigation.  All occupants were detained as part of the investigation and recovered firearm. Released 10/19/2016 at 4:15 AM by Lt. Anthony Fiore
