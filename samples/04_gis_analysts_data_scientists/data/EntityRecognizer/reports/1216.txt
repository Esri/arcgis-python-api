A local pizza delivery driver was making a delivery on the 2500 block of Calypso Road when he encountered two suspects, one of whom pointed a long rifle at him. The suspects demanded cash and the pizzas. The victim was not injured. The suspects remain at large. Anyone with information on this incident is encouraged to call the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crimestoppers.  Released 08/23/2018 at 1:14 AM by Sgt. Nathaniel Lujan
