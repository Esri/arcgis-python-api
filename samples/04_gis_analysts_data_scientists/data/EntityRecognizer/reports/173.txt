 A large amount of cash was stolen from a parked Jeep Monday morning after a thief smashed out a window and grabbed bags of money from the front seat.  It was parked just a few feet from the from the entrance to the Mobil gas station, 3019 E. Washington Ave., and its owner had just left the vehicle. His job is to collect bank deposits from several area Mobil stations, and he was in the middle of his route, in the process of getting the weekend's receipts from the E. Washington location, when the crime occurred. Anyone with information is urged to contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014. Released 07/12/2016 at 12:02 PM by PIO Joel Despain
