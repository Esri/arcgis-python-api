While Madison Police were investigating the robbery at Selective Video a 2nd armed robbery occurred at the Red Letter News on East Washington Ave.   The suspect in this robbery was also described as a black male, about 6'00",  goatee, grey jacket with other colors mixed in and dark pants.  He was also armed with a handgun and took cash from the register before fleeing on foot from the area. If you have any information about these robberies please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers.  Released 05/08/2017 at 2:27 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
