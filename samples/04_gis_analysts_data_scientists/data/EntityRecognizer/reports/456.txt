The MPD and Monona Police responded to a the 6400 block of Bridge Rd. early this morning after a resident awoke and saw someone slashing tires in an apartment complex parking lot. The vandal was described as wearing dark clothing and walking away from the area. Officers found more than 20 damged vehicles. Anyone with information is urged to contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014.  Released 02/01/2016 at 9:53 AM by PIO Joel Despain
