 Madison Police were called at 01:34 to the area of East Washington and N. Blount Street for an armed robbery.   A 24 year old male was approached in the 100 block of N. Blount Street by a male with a black handgun. A 2nd male suspect then took his wallet.  The suspect with the gun was described as a black male, mid 20's, gray hoodie and blue jeans.  The two suspects were last seen running north on Blount toward Mifflin Street.  Investigation continuing at this time. If you have any information of this robbery please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers. Released 01/21/2018 at 3:29 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
