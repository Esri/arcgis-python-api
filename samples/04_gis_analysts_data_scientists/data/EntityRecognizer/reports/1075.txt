 The MPD is seeking citizen help in locating a missing Madison man. Jeremiah X. Yang, age 23, was last seen in the W. Badger Rd. corridor around 9:00 p.m. Monday night. Concerned family members contacted the MPD Tuesday night and a South District detective is currently leading a search for Jeremiah. There is no reason to believe he is the victim of foul play, but family and friends are concerned for his welfare. Jeremiah is around 5'6" and 185 pounds. He was last known to be wearing a black jacket over a gray sweatshirt, dark jeans and black boots. Anyone with information on his whereabouts is asked to contact Det. Roger Baker at 608-576-2711 or the Dane County Public Safety Communications (911) at 608-255-2345. Released 08/23/2017 at 1:04 PM by PIO Joel Despain
