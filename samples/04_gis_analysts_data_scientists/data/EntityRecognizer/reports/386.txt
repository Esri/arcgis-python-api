The Madison Police responded to the area of Monroe St and West Lawn Ave for the report of a strong armed robbery.  The victim stated he was walking home when he was jumped by the above subject.  The male did sustain some facial injuries from being attacked.  The suspect took the victim's wallet.  The suspect has not been identified. This investigation is ongoing.  If anyone has any information reference this incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department of the Madison area Crimestoppers program.
