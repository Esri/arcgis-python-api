 A Madison Metro driver was cited for a red signal violation following a bus versus car collision at the intersection of Ingersoll St. and East Washington Ave. Tuesday afternoon. The bus - which had no passengers - was traveling north on Ingersoll. The driver of the car - a 60-year-old man - was inbound on East Washington. Following the crash, he was transported to a hospital with minor injuries. Released 04/19/2017 at 11:44 AM by PIO Joel Despain
