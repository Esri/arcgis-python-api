In the last 24 hours MPD Officers have investigated 5 separate incidents of people being shot with paintballs.  The shootings that occurred on 8/6 took place at around 11pm, in the downtown area.  Today's incidents all took place on the eastside between 4:30pm and 5:30pm. Four of the victims that were struck with paintballs had minor injuries.  It is possible that the suspect(s) may be operating a blue or black vehicle with dark tinted windows.  If you have any suspect information, please contact the non-emergency dispatch number at 255-2345.   Released 08/07/2017 at 9:48 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
