 A convenience store clerk was robbed at gunpoint Wednesday night, and struck in the shoulder with the weapon. The holdup took place at the 7-Eleven, 2703 W. Beltline Highway just before 11:00 p.m. The victim was shaken but not injured. The crook was last seen running south on Todd Dr. Released 12/14/2017 at 9:28 AM by PIO Joel Despain
