The Madison Police were called this morning to the EconoLodge reference an armed robbery.  A female resident of the hotel reported that a male acquaintance who had been in her room had taken an undisclosed amount of money from her.  The male had a handgun with him but the weapon was not brandished at the victim.  The victim was not injured. The Madison Police are continuing to investigate this incident and encourage anyone with information regarding it to contact the Madison Police or Madison area Crimestoppers.  Released 08/14/2018 at 4:03 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
