 Madison Police responded to an armed robbery at Selective Video on Commercial Avenue.  A male suspect came into the store and pulled a handgun on the clerk, putting it to her back. The suspect took money from the cash register after ordering it to be opened.  The suspect then fled on foot from the scene with an undetermined amount of cash.  No injuries were reported at this time. If you have any information about this robbery please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers. Released 05/08/2017 at 2:16 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
