Madison Police Responded to the 3000 Block of Darbo Drive shortly after 12:30PM for a reported stabbing.  A 16 year old female got into an argument with her 24 year old sister. After damaging a mirror in the home, the 16 year old used a piece of broken glass to stab her sister in the arm several times.  The 24 year old victim was taken to a local hospital with non life threatening injuries.  Madison Police Officers were able to take the suspect into custody without incident and she is currently in the Dane County Juvenile Reception Center.  Released 11/25/2016 at 2:25 PM by Sgt. Nicholas Ellis
