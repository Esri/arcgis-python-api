On 9/15/17 at approximately 12:08 am Madison police officers were dispatched to an armed robbery at the Wendy's Restaurant located in the 2400 block of S. Stoughton Rd.  Present at the time were employees who were ordered down to the ground by the suspect at gun point.  The suspect had his face covered during the robbery.  After ordering the employees down the suspect then ordered one of the employees to take him to the safe where he stole an undisclosed amount of cash as well as some of that employee's property.  At that point the suspect fled the scene.  MPD's investigation is ongoing. Released 09/15/2017 at 1:56 AM by Sgt. Kurt Wege
