Shortly after 11:00 AM, MPD responded to a report of property damage due to shots fired, in the 2000 block of McKenna Blvd.  On arrival, officers found that at least one building had been struck by gunfire, and several casings were located nearby.  It is believed that the damage occurred sometime overnight. There were no reports of injuries, and MPD does not believe that the resident of the struck building was targeted.  This incident is under investigation. Released 10/08/2016 at 8:21 PM by Sgt. Edward Marshall
