 OnStar GPS technology put the MPD in position Tuesday Morning to recover a stolen handgun and arrest a suspect on tentative weapons and drug charges. Police were notified by OnStar that a stolen car was parked behind a Schroeder Rd. apartment building. The suspect was inside the car. He had individually packaged baggies of heroin and a large amount of cash in his pockets. A handgun, listed as stolen out of Sun Prairie, was also recovered from the car's center console. Released 06/28/2017 at 1:01 PM by PIO Joel Despain
