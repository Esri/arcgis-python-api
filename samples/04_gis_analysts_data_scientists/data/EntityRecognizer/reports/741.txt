 A woman, removing groceries from her car in the 200 block of N. Paterson St., suffered leg injuries - including a break to one - when she was struck by a hit-and-run driver this afternoon. Witnesses said a silver Toyota sedan backed into the victim, pinning her between the Toyota and the injured woman's parked car. Anyone with information is encouraged to contact Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 266-6014.  Released 10/16/2017 at 2:38 PM by PIO Joel Despain
