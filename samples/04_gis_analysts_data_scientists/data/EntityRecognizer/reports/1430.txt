The Madison Police Department responded to the 600 block of Braxton Place after a report of a stabbing.  A male victim was found with serious injuries, and he is being treated at a local hospital.  The female suspect was located and ultimately arrested for this incident.  All of the parties in this incident have therefore been contacted and there is no outstanding danger to the public. Released 08/04/2018 at 3:12 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
