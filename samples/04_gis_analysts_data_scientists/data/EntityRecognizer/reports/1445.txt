 Several people called 911 to report possible shots being fired early this morning near the 1400 block of Spaight St. Responding officers could find no damage, no injured people, and no shell casings. There were many similar calls to dispatch this weekend regarding possible gunshots: 6500 block Bettys Lane on 4/29 at 5:19 a.m. 200 block Oak Street on 4/30 at 12:53 a.m. 1400 block E. Main St. on 4/30 at 1:28 a.m. Chicago Ave. at Fair Oaks on 4/30 at 2:07 a.m. 3400 block Graceland Ave. on 4/30 at 2:28 a.m. Officers responding to these locations did not immediately find any evidence and there were no reports of injuries or property damage.   Released 05/01/2017 at 10:11 AM by PIO Joel Despain
