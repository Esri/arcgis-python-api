 Officers recovered multiple casings and found two parked vehicles that had been damaged by gunfire after shots were fired early this morning in the parking lot of the Econo Lodge, 4726 East Washington Ave. Witnesses reported seeing two men fighting prior to the gun violence. Officers canvassed hotel rooms making sure there were no gunshot victims. None were found. Those involved in the dispute fled prior to police arrival. Released 07/24/2017 at 9:14 AM by PIO Joel Despain
