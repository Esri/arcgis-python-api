A resident in the 5400 block of Commercial Ave. called police because he located a bullet fragment inside of his house.  The front window of the residence was damaged as well.  The resident reported that they believed the damage occurred between Sunday night and Monday morning earlier this week.  The residence was occupied during the timeframe when the damage occurred but there were no reported injuries.  There are no suspects at this time. Released 08/15/2018 at 8:27 PM by Lt. Jamar Gary
