on 05/25/18 at approximately 12:37 AM Madison officers were called to the area of Tokay Blvd and Charles Lane for reports of shots fired. Officers located fired cartridge casings in the roadway at this intersection. A dark-in-color sedan was seen fleeing the area. Officers did not locate any property damage, and no persons were reportedly injured. Released 05/25/2018 at 3:39 AM by Lt. Gregory Esser
