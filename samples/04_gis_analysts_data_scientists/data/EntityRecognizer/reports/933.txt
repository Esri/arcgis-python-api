 An trespassing investigation evolves into a drug and gun case last night after officers contact a man who was loitering in the parking lot of Walgreens, 5702 Raymond Rd. Officers were conducting surveillance due to past drug trafficking and approached the suspect after observing him for a period of time. While arresting him for trespassing, they searched his backpack. It contained a handgun and a bag of oxycodone pills. The Waunakee teen was arrested on tentative charges listed above. Released 06/03/2016 at 10:45 AM by PIO Joel Despain
