 A 59-year-old Dane County man was arrested for his 7th offense of drunken driving early this morning. A witness said he was driving his car in the wrong lanes of traffic on University Ave., near N. Segoe Rd., going westbound in the eastbound lanes, when he collided head-on with an oncoming vehicle. There were no serious injuries. A partially consumed case of beer was found in the trunk of the suspect's car. Released 04/27/2016 at 3:16 PM by PIO Joel Despain
