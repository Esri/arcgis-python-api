On 10/18/16 at approximately 3:39 p.m. Madison Police Officers were alerted to a female needing help.  As the investigation continued, Officers and Detectives learned of a potential human trafficking case and rescued the adult, female victim from the conditions she was surviving under.  A Detective was assigned the case and a search warrant was executed 10/20/16 producing information leading to the arrest of Wise and Bailey for "keeping a place of prostitution".  Both individuals were arrested on scene, on 10/20, without incident.  This remains and ongoing investigation by the Madison Police Department in which additional charges could be forthcoming with more individuals.  Released 10/21/2016 at 9:24 AM by Lt. Michael Hanson
