Suspect entered Azara Hookah at 429 State Street and began concealing various merchandise. An employee attempted to stop the suspect when they tried to exit with the unpaid merchandise. The suspect then pulled out a knife and swung the knife at the employee. The suspect left the store and ran towards University Ave. The employee was not injuried and the suspect is still at large.
