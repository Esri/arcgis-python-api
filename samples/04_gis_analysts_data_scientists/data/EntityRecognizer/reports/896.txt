 A smash-and-grab burglar made off with a large quantity of alcohol and cigarettes during an overnight break-in at Northside Liquor, 2801 N. Sherman Ave. A broken window was discovered around 5:30 a.m. by a MPD officer who was in the area on routine patrol. A rock was found inside the store. A glass door to a nearby address was also shattered. This is an empty business property that is currently for lease. Released 11/13/2018 at 10:35 AM by PIO Joel Despain
