A man identified later as Nathaniel Pope allegedly stole liquor from Trixie's Liquor then fled in a vehicle prior to officers arriving. Pope completed an illegal u-turn at Hwy 51 and Hwy 30 and was struck by a vehicle that did have the right-of-way. Pope fled on foot, climbed a fence and was apprehended by officers near Walmart.
