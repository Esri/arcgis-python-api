Multiple callers report hearing approximately 2 gunshots in this area.  Upon investigation, it was determined that a victim was reportedly sleeping in his apartment when he was struck by an unknown suspect with a hard object, believed to be a gun.  Victim fought with the attacker, causing that suspect and another to flee the residence.  Shell casings were recovered inside the residence.  At the time of this report, no gunshot-related injuries have been discovered or reported, however the victim did receive a minor non-gunshot-related wound.  Investigation is continuing. Released 11/22/2017 at 3:45 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
