 The Burglary Crime Unit is investigating a break-in from this past weekend where 12 cell phones - most iPhone X models - were stolen from the U.S. Cellular kiosk at East Towne Mall. The phones, worth thousands of dollars, were discovered missing Saturday morning. A cabinet that had housed them was pried open. Released 12/22/2017 at 10:51 AM by PIO Joel Despain
