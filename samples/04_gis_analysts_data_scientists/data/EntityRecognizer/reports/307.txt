Officers were dispatched to a hold-up alarm at the Associated Bank, at 448 S Gammon Rd.   A female suspect entered the bank at around 2:55pm, and demanded money from the teller.  The suspect was wearing multicolored pajama pants and a gray and black hooded sweatshirt.  She was described as being approximately 5'6" tall with a medium build. The suspect left the bank with an undisclosed amount of money.  It is believed that the suspect left the area in a vehicle.  No one was injured during the robbery.  Anyone with information is asked to call Madison Area Crimestoppers at 266-6014.  Released 12/05/2016 at 4:50 PM by Lt. Kelly Donahue
