On April 18, 2019 at approximately 10:09 pm, Madison Police Officers were dispatched to an Armed Robbery that occurred at the BP Gas Station (6202 Schroeder Road, Madison, Wisconsin). The suspect implied he was armed with a handgun, confronted the clerk, took money from the register, and fled on foot with an undisclosed amount of currency. After arriving on scene, officers attempted a K9 track but the suspect was not located. Anyone with information about this crime is encouraged to call Madison Area Crime Stoppers at 608-266-6014. Released 04/19/2019 at 12:33 AM by Sgt. Nathan Becker
