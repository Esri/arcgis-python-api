 Madison Police responded to an armed robbery at the T-Mobile store on Dempsey Road at 19:02.  Three masked suspects entered the store and at least one was armed with a handgun.  The suspects took over $20,000 worth of phones and cell equipment before fleeing from this location. Officers were able to develop and locate a total of four suspects as well as the vehicle they were in. The suspects were taken into custody south of Madison near County HWY N and H. A handgun and evidence of the crime were found with the suspects.   All suspects are being charged in connection to this armed robbery. The investigation is continuing at this time.  Released 01/13/2018 at 12:14 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
