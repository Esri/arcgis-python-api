 A woman stabbed her boyfriend in the torso inside their Churchill Dr. home Sunday afternoon. Investigating officers determined it was an act of self-defense as he was shoving the woman at the time of the incident. She provided potentially life-saving aid to the man prior to the arrival of paramedics. The injured man was taken to a hospital where he was to undergo surgery. When released he will be arrested for domestic disorderly conduct. Released 11/06/2017 at 11:16 AM by PIO Joel Despain
