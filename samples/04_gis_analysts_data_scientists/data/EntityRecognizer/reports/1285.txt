On 08/10/15, around 10:30 pm a victim was grabbed by a male suspect who held a knife to the victim's stomach.  The suspect did not speak English therefore the victim was unable to understand what the suspect was saying.  The victim screamed at which time the suspect punched the victim and fled on foot.  The victim did not sustain any injuries as a result of the assault.  Investigation is on-going. Released 08/11/2015 at 5:09 AM by Lt. Lori Chalecki
