Madison Police officers were called to the Journey Seafood and Sushi Restaurant reference a strong armed robbery this evening.  The victim was walking to her car in the parking lot when she was approached by the suspect described above.  The suspect grabbed the victim's purse, and after a struggle was able to wrench it free from her.  During the struggle, the suspect struck the victim and also threatened her.  The victim was not seriously injured. If anyone has any information about this incident, they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program. Released 11/10/2018 at 1:58 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
