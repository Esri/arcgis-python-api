MPD responded to a report of shots fired at 1865 Northport Drive.  Upon arrival, casings were located as well as damage to a building caused by a vehicle crash.  A vehicle was found nearby with heavy damage and was determined to be involved.  The occupants were found and contacted, and said they were chased by a gold Crown Victoria that fired rounds at them.  The vehicle was hit by gunfire.  Investigation is ongoing. Released 06/22/2018 at 12:51 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
