The Madison Police are asking for assistance in locating a missing juvenile from the City of Madison's South Side. 15 year old female Binta M Jammeh was last seen earlier in the week at West High School. Jammeh left school mid-day and has not been heard from or seen since the 21st of November. Please contact the Madison Police Department if you have information on Jammeh's whereabouts. Released 11/24/2018 at 3:41 PM by Sgt. Nicholas Ellis
