On 11/13/18 at 11:42 PM the Kwik Trip located in the 3100 block of Maple Grove Dr. on Madison's west side was robbed.  The male suspect entered the store, dressed all in black and wearing a face mask, implied he had a gun and demanded money from the store employee.  The suspect then fled the area on foot with an undisclosed amount of money.  A K9 track was completed but officers were unable to locate the suspect.  Released 11/14/2018 at 12:56 AM by Sgt. Kurt Wege
