Last evening after 08:30 PM multiple Police Agencies received reports of individuals in a black Chevrolet Equinox shooting paintball guns at homes, and other property. The City of Madison Police Department fielded multiple calls regarding this activity on Madison's east and south sides. Area agencies received calls about the same vehicle and activity to include Fitchburg and McFarland Police. There were no reports of people being shot. MPD continues to investigate. Released 06/03/2018 at 8:56 AM by Sgt. Eric Tripke
