 A Dane County woman was found deceased in Lake Mendota early this morning after a concerned citizen called 911 regarding a vehicle submerged in water very near the boat launch at Spring Harbor Park, 5218 Lake Mendota Dr. Dive teams from both the Madison Fire Department and the Dane County Sheriff's Office assisted at the scene. The Dane County Medical Examiner and MPD detectives are working together on the death investigation. MPD patrol officers canvassed the neighborhood seeking information on the case.  Released 11/28/2016 at 2:31 PM by PIO Joel Despain
