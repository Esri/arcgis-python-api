 A man with a past history of committing burglaries was arrested again early Sunday morning as he walked away from Pooley's Sports Bar, 5441 High Crossing Blvd.The owner of the business knew a burglary was in progress thanks to surveillance images that he can view on his phone. He arrived to show officers the video.Officers realized they had just past the suspect on their way to the scene, and went back to take him into custody. Cash was stolen during the burglary.  Released 12/17/2018 at 11:16 AM by PIO Joel Despain
