 A 59-year-old Madison man suffered numerous broken bones after getting hit by a car while crossing Williamson St. at S. Thornton Ave. Friday night. He was cited for sudden pedestrian movement into traffic. The car's driver, a 17-year-old Madisonian said the man darted in front of his car as he was driving about 25 mph, and he had no time to brake before impact. The teen was very concerned about the welfare of the injured man. Released 01/08/2018 at 9:44 AM by PIO Joel Despain
