 Madison Police are currently investigating an incident in the 1900 block of Prairie Road where a shot was fired in a residence and some type of disturbance occurred. No one was hurt from the gunfire but a person was injured after being struck with an unknown object.  Detectives, investigator and officers are on scene and investigation is continuing as multiple people were at this location at the time of the incident. Released 11/14/2017 at 10:29 PM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
