The Madison Police Department responded to the 1300 block of Ruskin Street regarding an armed robbery.  The victim is a delivery driver and responded to that address per the ordering party.  Upon arrival he was met by suspect #1, who attempted to pay with a counterfeit bill.  As the victim confronted suspect #1 on this fact, the additional two suspects walked up.  One lifted his shirt which displayed the handle of a handgun in his waistband.  The suspects then ran from the area after receiving an undisclosed amount of cash. This investigation is ongoing.  Anyone with information regarding this incident is encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crimestoppers.  Released 06/27/2018 at 1:25 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
