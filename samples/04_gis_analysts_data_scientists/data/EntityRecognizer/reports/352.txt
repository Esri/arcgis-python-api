Meriter Hospital contacted the Madison Police Department this evening reference a male who arrived at their emergency room with a gunshot wound.  Upon arrival, officers were able to determine that this injury was inflicted within the City of Madison.  During the initial investigation, it was determined that the incident occurred in the 2100 block of Luann Lane.  The victim suffered a non-life threatening injury.  There is very limited suspect information at this time but the initial investigation has shown that this appears to be a targeted event.  The Madison Police will continue to investigate this incident but if anyone has any information regarding it they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police or the Madison area Crimestoppers program. Released 11/11/2018 at 1:02 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
