 A 56-year-old Fitchburg woman was cited for failure to yield and driving with a suspended license Friday night after she struck three children who were in a crosswalk and crossing Post Rd. at Traceway Dr. The children who were 11 to 13 years old suffered non life-threatening injuries. Two were transported to a hospital. A third did not need medical attention. The driver stayed at the scene and was cooperative. Released 06/24/2017 at 9:37 PM by PIO Joel Despain
