The Madison Police were dispatched to the 1600 block of Troy Drive reference an armed robbery and theft of auto.  The female victim was exiting her vehicle when she was confronted by 3 suspects, one of which was armed with a black handgun.  The suspects took personal property from the victim, including her keys, and then took her vehicle.  The victim was not injured. The vehicle is a silver 2010 Honda Fit, and it was last seen headed outbound on Northport Drive.  Anyone with information regarding this incident is encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison Area Crimestoppers.  Released 08/28/2018 at 2:01 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
