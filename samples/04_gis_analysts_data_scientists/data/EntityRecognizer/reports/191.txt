 A Madison Police officer suffered minor injuries Tuesday afternoon while arresting a combative woman who had been exposing herself to motorists on busy E. Washington Ave. The woman was also running into traffic. When contacted by the MPD, she scratched the officer's face and arms, while also pulling the officer's hair. The woman was taken to jail and mental health professionals were contacted. Released 10/05/2016 at 12:25 PM by PIO Joel Despain
