The eastbound lanes of Highway 30, between Interstate 90/94 and Highway 51, are closed due to a serious car versus pedestrian crash. Early reports indicate a woman suffered life-threatening injuries after being struck. The eastbound lanes could remain closed for a couple of hours and motorists are advised to use alternative routes. Additional details on the crash will be provided as more is known. Released 06/22/2016 at 3:21 PM by PIO Joel Despain
