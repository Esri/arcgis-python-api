Madison Police officers were called at 5:41pm to the Chase Bank located at 4513 Milwaukee St for an armed robbery in progress. Two black males as described above entered the bank and ordered people to the floor at gunpoint. The suspects left the bank on foot and were given an undetermined amount of money. A K9 track was conducted, but did not yield the suspects. No injuries were reported as a result of this robbery and the investigation is continuing at this time. If you have any information about this robbery, please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers. Released 01/26/2018 at 6:43 PM by Lt. Mindy Winter
