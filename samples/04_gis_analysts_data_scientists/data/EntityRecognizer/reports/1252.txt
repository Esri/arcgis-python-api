A burglar entered a S. Owen Dr. home through an unlocked window sometime overnight while a family slept. The intruder stole a laptop and other electronics. The thief also took keys to the family's SUV and drove off in it. The crime was detected around 4:30 a.m. when a family member got up to get ready for work.   Released 03/29/2017 at 2:53 PM by PIO Joel Despain
