 Madison Police were called to an armed robbery that occurred at 200 Langdon Street.  Two suspects were found going through the house taking items and when confronted by a resident the suspects displayed a gun before fleeing the area.  The suspects were described as darker skin males, possible Indian or Middle Eastern descent, both wearing dark hoodies, sweatpants, 20-25 years old, with one having a beard.  A canine track was completed and cameras were being checked in the area of this robbery.  No one was injured and investigation is continuing. Please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers if you have any information about these suspects.    Released 10/30/2018 at 2:44 AM by Sgt. Paul Jacobsen
