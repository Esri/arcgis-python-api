A caller reported a suspicious package outside of the Target store at Hilldale Mall.  Madison Police, Madison Fire, and DCSO are on scene and investigating.  Several roadways and driveways around Hilldale are closed pursuant to this call, and MPD is currently asking motorists to avoid the area for the time being.  This incident is currently under investigation. Released 07/09/2016 at 8:28 PM by Sgt. Edward Marshall
