The Madison Police responded to this location reference a carjacking that had occurred.  The four victims were in a vehicle when two African American males, one armed with a handgun, opened up the doors and demanded they exit the car.  Everyone exited and the suspects took the car from the area.  None of the occupants of the car were injured. The car was recovered by the Madison Police and the investigation is ongoing at this time.  Released 12/06/2018 at 11:25 PM by Lt. Daniel Nale
