Delivery driver was delivering $37 worth of food to an address to a customer who also requested the driver bring change for a large denomination bill.  Upon arrival, the driver observed the above described suspects who asked if he had the food and the change.  When the driver produced both, one suspect punched the delivery driver, taking the change and the food.  All suspects then fled the immediate area. Released 04/21/2017 at 1:00 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
