The Madison Police Department was dispatched to a report of an attempted strong armed robbery of a pizza delivery driver in the 2700 block of Dryden Drive.  The female victim, who was not hurt, stated that the above described suspect demanded cash from her.  He ran from the area when she started yelling for help. This investigation is ongoing.  If anyone has any information regarding this incident they are encouraged to contact the Madison Police Department or the Madison area Crimestoppers program.  Released 09/26/2018 at 1:27 AM by Lt. Daniel Nale
