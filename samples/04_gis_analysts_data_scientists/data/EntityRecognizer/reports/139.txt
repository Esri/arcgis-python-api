MPD received information from a single caller stating they had heard what they believed to be gunshots in approximately the 7500 block of Mid Town Rd, believing the also saw muzzle flashes.  Officers responded and located multiple shell casings in the road in this area.  There were no buildings, people, or other vehicles located in the immediate area the casings were located, and no damage or injured persons were located in this location.  At the time of this release it is uncertain what, if anything, was the target of this gunfire. Released 03/22/2017 at 4:58 AM by Lt. Timothy Radke
