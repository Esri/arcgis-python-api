 A Madison man needed stitches to close a couple of cuts after being hit with a beer bottle Saturday evening. The victim had been is a dispute with another man in the 2900 block of Turbot Dr. when the suspect smashed the bottle over victim's head. The suspect has not yet been located. There is probable cause to arrest him for substantial battery and disorderly conduct.     Released 07/16/2018 at 10:23 AM by PIO Joel Despain
