On April 18th, 2017, around 6:00 p.m., an attempted abduction occurred on Mt. Vernon Court.  A child was playing in her yard when a black male grabbed her and attempted to take her away.  Family members saw what was happening and quickly intervened.  The man let go of the child and fled.  Released 04/18/2017 at 8:56 PM by Sgt. Jennifer Ryan
