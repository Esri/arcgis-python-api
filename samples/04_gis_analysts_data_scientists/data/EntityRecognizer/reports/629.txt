Madison Police Officers were dispatched to the Jiffy Lube at 3140 Maple Valley Drive for a reported intoxicated driver in the parking lot. Officers contacted Michael Peterson, Madison, WI who refused to perform field sobriety testing. Peterson was arrested for 7th Offense OMVWI and taken to the Dane County Jail. Released 05/18/2019 at 5:02 PM by Sgt. Nicholas Ellis
