 A Madison man, out of parole for a previous heroin delivery case, is in jail again. Thursday morning members of MPD SWAT, and the MPD Gang Unit joined the Dane County Narcotics Task Force in serving a no-knock warrant at the suspect's McKenna Blvd. apartment. A handgun, more than 200 rounds of ammunition, a high capacity handgun magazine, drugs and other contraband were seized.
