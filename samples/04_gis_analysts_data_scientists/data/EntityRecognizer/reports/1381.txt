The MPD confiscated a pellet gun from a 13-year-old student this morning after receiving information that the teen had threatened to harm another student at Sherman Middle School, 1610 Ruskin St. The student had concealed the pellet gun in his pants. He was taken to the Juvenile Reception Center on a tentative charge of having a firearm on school grounds. Released 10/05/2017 at 2:34 PM by PIO Joel Despain
