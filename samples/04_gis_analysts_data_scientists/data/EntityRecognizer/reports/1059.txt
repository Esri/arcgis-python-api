 The MPD caught a suspect in the act of stealing hundreds of dollars worth of Magic collectors' cards early Sunday morning after being dispatched to a burglary in progress. An assistant manager at Misty Mountain Games, 4672 Cottage Grove Rd., was at home when his phone alerted him motion being detected inside the store. He called 911 as the store's owner was then able observe the burglar at work from surveillance camera feeds. Officers set up a perimeter and spotted a man inside. He was dressed in black and carrying a backpack. The suspect complied with officers' commands. The trading cards were found in his pack. He gained entry by breaking out a window. A hammer was found outside near shattered glass. Released 12/05/2016 at 10:22 AM by PIO Joel Despain
