MPD officers responded to Wayne's Automotive reference an armed robbery.  The clerk reported that the robber had been in the shop for a time waiting for a ride.  The robber attempted to pay for items but when the cash register was opened, the robber told the clerk to keep the drawer open because it was a robbery.  The suspect then fled with an undisclosed amount of cash.  No injuries were reported.  Investigation ongoing. Released 11/28/2018 at 8:58 PM by Lt. Jamar Gary
