Responding to community concerns, the Dane County Narcotics Task Force joined with the North District's Community Police Team and the MPD Gang Unit for a proactive initiative last Friday night targeting street level drug dealing in the E. Washington Ave. and Northport Dr. corridors. Numerous contacts, stops and arrests were made. The team also assisted North Patrol officers with a car versus pedestrian crash. Crack cocaine, marijuana, cash, cell phones and four counterfeit $100 bills were seized during the operation.    Released 04/26/2018 at 10:10 AM by PIO Joel Despain
