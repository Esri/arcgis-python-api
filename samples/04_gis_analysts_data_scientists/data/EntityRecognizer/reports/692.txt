 The MPD is on the scene of a shooting where a teenager suffered a non-life-threatening gunshot wound this morning. Callers to 911 reported a fight between several young men at the corner of Linda Vista Rd. and Turner Ave. around 10:40 a.m. Witnesses also reported at least one gun being fired. The wounded boy was found walking in the area. Officers are in the process of interviewing witnesses. The area of the shooting is currently closed to traffic. Released 09/26/2018 at 11:03 AM by PIO Joel Despain
