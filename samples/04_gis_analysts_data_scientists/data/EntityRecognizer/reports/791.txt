The MPD and the Dane County Medical Examiner are investigating the death of a man found inside his Mason St. home by a relative this afternoon. The deceased was in his 90's. A number of MPD resources responded to the scene prompting media calls. The Medical Examiner will determine the cause of death. If it is deemed suspicious, more information will be released by the MPD.   Released 01/21/2016 at 3:14 PM by PIO Joel Despain
