A Madison woman said she was groped by a photographer during a photo shoot at his Atwood Avenue studio Friday afternoon. The 20-year-old works at a restaurant, and told police the photographer was a customer who had offered to have her come to his business for the purpose of building a portfolio. The woman said besides taking clothed photos of her, the man came on to her and touched her inappropriately. He denied doing anything that was not consensual.
