Inbound lanes of Mineral Point Rd. are currently shut down at S. Segoe Rd. following a crash involving a vehicle and a bicyclist. Preliminary information indicates the biker, a man in his 70's, suffered life-threatening injuries and is in critical condition. Midtown officers and a MPD Traffic Specialist are on scene. Motorists should use alternative routes. Released 09/17/2018 at 10:58 AM by PIO Joel Despain
