Madison police responded to the report of a strong armed robbery outside of Visions Night Club.  The victim states that the suspect approached him, snatched his wallet, and fled the area.  Suspect has not been located at this time.  Anyone with additional informatioin please contact Madison Police Crimestoppers at 266-6014.
