 A 65-year-old bicyclist suffered non life-threatening injuries after being struck by a small SUV at the intersection of Aberg Ave. and Shopko Dr. around 7:30 a.m. The injured man said he had come off a bike trail, and had pushed a button for a walk light in order to cross Aberg Ave.  The crash occurred as he entered the roadway. The accident is under investigation and no citations have been issued.  Released 03/21/2017 at 9:33 AM by PIO Joel Despain
