 A Madison Police officer was punched in the face after responding to Demetral Field, 601 N. Sixth St., last night. A citizen had called about an unconscious man, and the officer went to check on his welfare. As she approached, the man took off running then reversed his course and attacked the officer. Madison Fire paramedics treated her injuries and the suspect was taken to jail. Released 09/15/2017 at 10:52 AM by PIO Joel Despain
