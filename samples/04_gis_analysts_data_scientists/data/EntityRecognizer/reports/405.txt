At 2:19 AM Madison police officers were sent to the 1700 block of Windom Way on Madison's north side for a report of an armed robbery.  When officers arrived they made contact with the 22 year old male victim who was a pizza delivery driver.  While attempting a delivery the driver was ordered at gunpoint to give up his money by the suspect.  The driver complied and the suspect was last seen running northbound along N. Sherman Ave.  The suspect was described as a black male in his early twenties who was approximately 5'10" and was wearing a gray sweatshirt.  There were no injuries reported during this incident and the investigation is continuing. Released 09/21/2018 at 3:56 AM by Sgt. Kurt Wege
