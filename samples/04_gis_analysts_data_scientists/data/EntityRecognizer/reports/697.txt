 A 56-year-old man was arrested for his seventh offense of drunken driving Monday afternoon after the truck he was driving crashed into another vehicle in the 1900 block of Kropf Ave. Citizens helped lead police to the location of the suspect, who was found in the 500 block of Dapin Rd., still inside the cab of the truck, which was now stopped. The driver recorded a .18 while taking a portable breathalyzer test. Released 09/06/2016 at 3:19 PM by PIO Joel Despain
