 A 22-year-old Madison man suffered a facial fracture and other head injuries after being struck several times with a socket wrench Wednesday afternoon. The attack happened in a Hayes Rd. parking lot. The victim knows the suspect and the MPD is in the process of trying to locate and arrest the other man on a tentative charge of substantial battery while armed.
