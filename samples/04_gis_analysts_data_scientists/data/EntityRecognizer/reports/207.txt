Madison Police were called for an armed robbery in the 1500 block of Troy Dr. at 23:55.  Three males approached the victim, a food delivery driver, shortly after the victim had arrived in the area for a delivery.  Two of these males each displayed a handgun and one male took cash from the driver. The only suspect descriptions were 3 black males, 15-16 years old wearing dark clothing.  They were last seen running towards an area business. Investigation continuing. If you have information about this robbery or these suspects please contact the Madison Police Department or Madison Area Crime Stoppers  Released 02/17/2018 at 3:10 AM by Sgt. Joseph Engler
